25. Dunkin Coconut declared a 1 million dividend on its common stocks. It there were 100,000 such shares, how much was the dividend per share for common stocks?


Answer:

The dividend per share for common stocks is 10.

Step-by-step explanation:

STEP 1: Analyze the problem.

Dunkin Coconut declared a 1 million dividend on its common stocks. It there were 100,000 such shares, how much was the dividend per share for common stocks?

Dividend = 1,000,000.00

Shares = 100,000.00

STEP 2: Use the dividend per share formula.

Dividend per share formula is

[tex]\frac{Annual dividend}{No.ofshares}[/tex]

[tex]Annual Dividend=\frac{1,000,000.00}{100,000.00}[/tex]

[tex]Annual Dividend=10[/tex]
