answer: I’d suggest you crank out one of the kinematics equations. Since you want s, the distance travelled, and you have u, the initial velocity, and a, the acceleration (this is g, which you might take as either -10 or -9.81: negative because the stone is travelling upwards and gravity is downwards), and t, the time taken, the obvious equation to use is s=ut+12at2 . Substituting (I’ll use a = -10), you get s = 45 - 45 = 0, so we find that we have travelled 0 metres altogether.

Explanation:

This means that our stone is back where it started, so it has reached a peak and fallen again. Absent air resistance, we can be happy that the highest point was reached halfway through this time, so that it spent just as much time falling as rising (which is how it ended up back at the same height as where it started). So find out how far it travelled in 1.5 seconds (which should be when it stopped), then double it.

Using the same formula, we now have s = 22.5 - 11.25 = 11.25, which when doubled gives us 22.5 metres.

25. Mother used 2 1/2 meters of cloth in sewing a blouse. How many boluses can she sew from a roll of cloth containing 20 1/3 meters?​


Answer:

8 [tex]\frac{2}{15}[/tex] blouses

Step-by-step explanation:

Division of mixed numbers

Mixed numbers are represented by a whole number with a proper fraction that gives value in between any two numbers. Solving mathematical problems such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division with mixed numbers follow certain rules.

Steps in dividing mixed numbers

Here are the simple steps on how to perform the division of mixed numbers.

1. Write the mixed numbers into improper fractions by multiplying the whole number by the denominator and adding the resulting value to the numerator.

2. Take the reciprocal of the improper fraction on the denominator.

3. Multiply the reciprocal to the improper fraction of the numerator.

4. Multiply the numerators and the denominators.

5. Simplify the answer and convert the resulting improper fraction into a mixed number. The quotient is the whole number while the remainder will be the new numerator of the mixed number.

In this problem, we are given 2 1/2 meters of cloth that can sew a blouse and a total of 20 1/3 meters of cloth. We need to calculate how many blouses can be sewn with the total length of the cloth.

Step 1: Determine the mathematical equation of the problem.

20 1/3 ÷ 2 1/2 =?

Step 2: Write the mixed number into an improper fraction and proceed to division.

20 [tex]\frac{1}{3}[/tex]   ÷ 2[tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex] =?

[tex]\frac{(20 x 3 ) + 1}{3}[/tex]   ÷  [tex]\frac{(2x2) + 1}{2}[/tex] =?

[tex]\frac{61}{3}[/tex] ÷ [tex]\frac{5}{2}[/tex] =?

[tex]\frac{61}{3}[/tex] x [tex]\frac{2}{5}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{122}{15}[/tex]

Step 3: Simplify the resulting improper fraction into a mixed number.

[tex]\frac{122}{15}[/tex] = 8 [tex]\frac{2}{15}[/tex]

Therefore, the mother can sew 8 [tex]\frac{2}{15}[/tex] blouses.
