23. in a map that used a scale of 1 cm :20 km, what is the distance in centimeter between manila and cavite if manila is 180 km away from cavite?


Use the proportion method to solve for the map scale distance (in centimeter):

Let x be the map scale for 180 km:

1 cm : 20 km = x cm : 180 km

(20 km) (x cm) = (1 cm)(180 km)

x = (180 cm·km) ÷ (20 km)

x = 90 cm

ANSWER: 90 cm.
