26. The banko sentral of the Philippines has released new 10- and 5-peso coins. as new design, they were inscribed with regular dodecagon and nonagon respectively. Find The difference of the sum of their interior angles.​


Answer:

formula for sum of interior angles is:

sum of interior angles = (n-2)x180

where n = # of sides of the polygon

for dodecagon, it is a 12 sided polygon, so n=12

sum of interior angles = (n-2)x180

sum of interior angles = (12-2)x180

sum of interior angles = (10)x180

sum of interior angles = 1800°

for nonagon, it is a 9 sided polygon, so n=9

sum of interior angles = (n-2)x180

sum of interior angles = (9-2)x180

sum of interior angles = (7)x180

sum of interior angles = 1260°

So their difference is 1800 - 1260 = 540°
