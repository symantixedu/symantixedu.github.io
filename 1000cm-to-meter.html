22. you supposed to make a pencil holder box made with square base and has a volume of 1000cm³. what will be the measure of the side of its base that will make its surface area less than 525cm²?​


Answer:

10cm

volume=1000cm^3

surface area=240cm^2

Step-by-step explanation:

Let s be the side length of the base, and h be the height of the box. The base area is then s², and the side area is 4(h·s), or 4hs. The total area is s² + 4hs, and equals 525, so we have

s² + 4hs - 525 = 0. Using the quadratic formula, we get  s=−2h+h2+525−−−−−−−√.  

If we restrict s and h to integer values, it works for h=2:  s=−2(2)+22+525−−−−−−−√=19.  

The total area is then 19² + 4(19)(2) = 361+152 = 513 cm².
