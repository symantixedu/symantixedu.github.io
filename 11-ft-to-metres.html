3. A cargo container is 50 ft long, 10 ft wide, and 8 ft tall. Find its volume in cubic yards and cubic metres.


The volume is about 1333.3333 cubic yards.

Explanation:

The equation for volume is as follows, where l is length, w is width, and h is height :

l ⋅ w ⋅ h = V

Knowing this, we can input the given length, width, and height from the equation:

50 ⋅ 10 ⋅ 8 = V

After simplification, volume is found to be 4000 cubic feet:

4000 = V

As the question demands the answer in cubic yards, the volume of 4000 feet must be converted. The ratio of feet to yards is below.

3 : 1

In other words, every yard is three feet.

To find the value of 4000 cubic feet in yards, we must divide by three. Y is yards.

=y

Therefore, the volume in cubic yards is roughly 1333.3333.

1333.3333 ≈ y
