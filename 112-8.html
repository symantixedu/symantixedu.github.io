Step-by-step explanation:

Solution:

An = A1 + (n - 1) d

112 = 8 + (n - 1) 4

112 = 8 + 4n - 4

112 = 4 + 4n

112 - 4 = 4n

108/4 = 4n/4

27 = n

PROBLEM

What is n if d =4, the first term is 8 and the last term is 112?

ANSWERThere are a total of 27 terms in the given Arithmetic Sequence

SOLUTION

[tex]a_{n} = a_{1} + (n - 1)(d) \\ 112 = 8 + (n - 1)(4) \\ 112 = 8 + 4n - 4 \\ 112 = 4n + 4 \\ 112 -4 = 4n \\ 108 = 4n \\\boxed{27 = n}[/tex]
