26. A B 1. What is 150% of 12? ​


Answer:

18

EXPLANATION:

Percent Value: 150%

Number: 12

150% of 12...

... is equivalent to multiplying them:

150% × 12 = 18

Detailed calculations below.

'Percent (%)' means 'out of one hundred':

150% = 150 'out of one hundred',

p% is read p 'percent',

150% = 150/100 = 150 ÷ 100

Calculate percentage:

150% × 12 =

150/100 × 12 =

(150 ÷ 100) × 12 =

150 × 12 ÷ 100 =

1,800 ÷ 100 =

18

Proof.

How do we check the result?

If 150% × 12 = 18 =>

Divide 18 by 12...

... And see if we get as a result: 150%

Note:

100/100 = 100 ÷ 100 = 100% = 1

Multiply a number by the fraction 100/100,

... and its value doesn't change.

n/100 = n%, any number.

Calculate the percent value:

18 ÷ 12 =

1.5 =

1.5 × 100/100 =

(1.5 × 100)/100 =

150/100 =

150%

So we have proven that the calculations are correct.
