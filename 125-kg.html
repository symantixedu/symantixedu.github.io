P = mv

Motorcycle :

Sniper motorcycle :

= 787.5 kg.m/s

24. A solution is made by dissolving 125 g of sodium chloride in 1.12 kg of water. What is the percent by mass?​


Answer:

percent by mass = 10.0%

Explanation:

Calculating the mass of the solution

mass solution = mass solute + mass solvent

mass solution = 125 g + 1120 g

mass solution = 1245 g

Calculating the percent by mass

percent by mass = (mass solute / mass solution) × 100

percent by mass = (125 g / 1245 g) × 100

percent by mass = 10.0%
