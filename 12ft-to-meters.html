18. A garden is in the shape of a square. It side measure 12ft. Find it's area.


Answer:

144 ft²

Step-by-step explanation:

Given: 12 ft side measurement of the square.

Missing: The area of the square shape garden.

Formula: A = s²

Process:

(1) Substitute the "s" with 12 ft.

= (12 ft)²

(2) Multiply 12 by itself, as it is squared.

= (12 ft)(12 ft)

(3) Multiply the two numbers to get the product.

= 144 ft²

Answer: The area of the squared-shaped garden is 144 ft².
