5. 28. A submarine starts out at 135 feet below sea level. It dives 240 feet before rising 307 feet.Represent the current depth of the submarine as an integer.​


Answer:

The submarine is at 67 feet below the sea level (-67)

Step-by-step explanation:

* Lets explain how to solve the problem

- The zero level is the sea level

- Any height below the sea level is negative

- Any height above the sea level is positive

- A submarine starts out at 135 feet below the sea level

∴ The submarine is at -135 feet

-  The submarine dives 239 feet before rising

∴ The submarine dives another -239

∴ The submarine is at -135 + -239 = -374 feet

* The submarine is at 374 feet below the sea level

- The submarine rising 307 feet

∴ The submarine dives 307 feet up

- Add the last position to the rising distance

∴ The current depth of the submarine = -374 + 307 = -67 feet

∴ The submarine is at 67 feet below the sea level

How do you find distance using angle of depression?

Measure the vertical distance, or difference in altitudes, a .

Measure the angle of depression, α .

25. Katrina Cloth needed is 36 inches which is costs 1.50 per inch. How much would be the cost of the fabric that measures 135 centimeters?​


Answer:

To convert 135 centimeters to inches, we need to divide by the conversion factor of 2.54 (since there are 2.54 centimeters in an inch):

135 centimeters / 2.54 = 53.15 inches (rounded to two decimal places)

So the length of fabric needed is 53.15 inches.

The cost per inch is given as $1.50, so the total cost of the fabric would be:

53.15 inches x $1.50/inch = $79.73

Therefore, the cost of the fabric that measures 135 centimeters is $79.73.
