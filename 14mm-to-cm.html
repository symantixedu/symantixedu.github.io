[tex]V = (\frac{\pi d^2}{4})h[/tex]

[tex]V = (\frac{\pi (7mm)^2}{4})(14mm)\\V = 538.78 mm^3[/tex]


To calculate the total surface area TSA of the cylinder, the following formula will be used


[tex]TSA = \frac{\pi d^2}{2} + \pi dh\\TSA = \frac{\pi (7mm)^2}{2} + \pi (7mm)(14mm)\\TSA = 384.85 mm^2[/tex]
