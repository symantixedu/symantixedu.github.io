Length= 50

30. 20% of 160 is 50% of what number? ​


Answer:

To solve the problem, we can first find 20% of 160 by multiplying 160 by 0.2:

[tex]\mathrm{20\% \ of\ 160 = 0.2 \times 160 = 32}[/tex]

Next, we want to find a number that is 50% of this value. Let's call this number "x". We can set up an equation:

[tex]x\mathrm{\ = 50\% \ of\ 32}[/tex]

We can convert 50% to a decimal by dividing by 100, which gives us:

[tex]x = \mathrm{0.5 \times 32}[/tex]

Simplifying the right side, we get:

[tex]\boxed{x = 16}[/tex]

Therefore, 20% of 160 is 50% of 16.
