The actual elapsed time and estimated elapsed time refer to the amount of time that has passed from the initial time to the final time. They can be found by subtracting the initial time from the final time. The only difference is for the actual elapsed time, you are just going to use the actual time to subtract while for the estimated elapsed time, you have to round off the actual time before you subtract.
