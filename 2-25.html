22. Find the ratio of 40 to centimeters to 5 meters. a.3/25 b.25/3 c.2/25 d.25/2


Answer:

Since 5 meters is 500 centimeters, then this means that the ratio is going to be 40 : 500, but we still need to simplify it to get the final answer.

First, let us find the GCF of 40 and 500.

The GCF of 40 and 500 is 20.

Divide 40 and 500 from the resulting GCF.

40 ÷ 20 = 2

500 ÷ 20 = 25

Rewriting this, it will be 2:25 (Letter C)
