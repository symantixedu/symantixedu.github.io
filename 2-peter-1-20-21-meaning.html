3. . Why is that in 2 Peter 1:20-21 said, Peter warned against attempting to interpret forthemselves the prophecies which were written under the inspiration of the Holy Spirit?​


Answer:

According to those verses, all of the words which are written in the Bible are not from the opinions of every prophet. It is because all of the prophets are guided and filled with the Holy Spirit while they are writing the books.

This means that the prophets did not prophecy based on their own understanding but by the guidance from the Holy Spirit. If we were to interpret the prophecies by our own understanding of it, then our interpretation may contradict to the interpretation of other people.

We know that the books in the Bible do not contradict each other because they are interconnected. So if we want to understand the prophecies written in the Bible, we should pray to God and ask for His mercy that He may also move us or guide us with His Holy Spirit.
