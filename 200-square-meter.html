10. Jess wants to refinish a circular table that is 3 meters in diameter.if the refinishing coast 200 pesos per square meter, how much will she spend?


Answer:

Jess wants to refinish a circular table that is 3 meters in diameter.if the refinishing cost 200 pesos per square meter, how much will she spend?

Jess will spend 1,413 pesos to refinish the table.

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked?

How much will she spend?

What are given?

diameter of the table - 3m

cost per square meter - 200 pesos

We have two steps to know the amount that Jess would spend to refinish the circular table.

Find the area of the table.Multiply the area by the cost per square unit.

Solution:

1. Finding the Area

The formula in finding the area of a circle is Area ○ = πr², where π has a constant value of 3.14 and r is the radius. If the given is the diameter, you can get the radius by dividing it by 2.

radius = 3 ÷ 2 = 1.5

Substitute the values.

Area ○ = πr²

= (3.14)(1.5m)²

= (3.14)(2.25m²)

= 7.065 m²

2. Multiply the area by the cost per square unit.

7.065 × 200 pesos = 1,413 pesos

Final Answer:

Jess would spend 1,413 pesos to refinish the table.

27. 2. If the area of a rectangular garden lot is 200 Sq. Meters, how many square decimeters of grass are needed to fill it? With solution. ​


To convert square meters to square decimeters, we need to multiply the number of square meters by 10,000 (since there are 10,000 square decimeters in a square meter).

So, the area of the rectangular garden lot in square decimeters would be:

200 sq. meters x 10,000 sq. decimeters/sq. meter = 2,000,000 sq. decimeters

Therefore, we would need 2,000,000 square decimeters of grass to fill the garden lot.
