26. Convert 23°C to 'FO A. 70.4O B.73.4O C.99O D. 44.7​


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Answer:

The answer will be letter B, or 73.4°F.

For the formula:

(n°C × 1.8) + 32 = n°F

Now, let us apply 23 degrees to the formula.

(23°C × 1.8) + 32

So, let us use the GEMDAS method, and it stands for:

G - Groupings (Consists of Parentheses, Brackets, and Curly Brackets)

E - Exponents

MD - Multiplication and Division

AS - Addition and Subtraction

Moving on to the solution that we talked about it a while ago:

(23°C × 1.8) + 32 -> (41.4) + 32

(41.4) + 32 -> 73.4
