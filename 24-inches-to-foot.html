29. _5. The shadow cast by a one-foot ruler is 8 inches long. At the same time, the shadow castby a pine tree is 24 feet longWhat is the height in feet of the pine tree?a. 3 feet1 foot24 feetb. 16 feetC. 36 feetd. 192 feet​


Answer:

Given:

*for ruler

h = 1 foot

l = 8 inches = 2/3 ft

*for pine tree

H = ?

L = 24 ft

Solution:

[tex] \frac{h}{l} = \frac{H}{L} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{1}{ \frac{2}{3} } = \frac{H}{24} [/tex]

[tex]H = \frac{(1)(24)}{ \frac{2}{3} } = \frac{(1)(24)(3)}{2} [/tex]

[tex]H = 36ft[/tex]
