27. (Post Test) Directions: Find the perimeter of the polygons below. FIGURE 2 km 4 km 3 km 4 km​


To find the perimeter of the polygon, we need to add up the length of all the sides.

In this case, the polygon has four sides with lengths 2 km, 4 km, 3 km, and 4 km.

Therefore, the perimeter of the polygon is:

2 km + 4 km + 3 km + 4 km = 13 km

So, the perimeter of the polygon is 13 km.
