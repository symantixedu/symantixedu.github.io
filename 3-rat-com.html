17. If 3 cats can kill 3 rats in 3 minutes. how long it will take 100 cats to kill 100 rats?


Answer:

1 hour and 40 minutesis the amount of time for the 100 cats to kill 100 rats.

Step-by-step explanation:

If 3 cats can kill 3 rats in 3 minutes,

Therefore 1 cat can kill 1 rat in 1 minute.

So if 100 cats will kill 100 rats

to find the answer, refer to the solution:

100 cats multiplied by 1 cat ( 100 cats × 1 minute per cat)

= 100 minutes=100 ÷ 60

(to find the remainder, subtract 60 to 100 so: 100-60= 40 )

=  1 Hour and 40 minutes.

(60 = the number of minutes in an hour.)

2nd:

[tex] \\ \sf{} = \frac{30}{30} \\ \\ \sf{} = 1 \: rat \: per \: minute \\ \\ [/tex]

[tex] \\ \sf{} 3 : 1 = x: 1 \\ \\ \sf{} \frac{3}{1} = \frac{x}{1} \\ \\ \sf{} 3 = x \\ \\ \huge \boxed{ \sf{} x = 3} \\ \\ \\ [/tex]
