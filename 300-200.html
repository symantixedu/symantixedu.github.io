4. Nine people gave contributions in pesos 100, 200, 100, 300, 300, 200, 200, 150, 100, and 100 for a door prize. what is the median contribution?


The median of the contribution that the nine people give for a door prize is 175.

What is the median?

The median in mathematics is the value that is right in the middle of the sets of data provided when they are arranged.

How do we find the median?

The median can be solved by arranging the sets of data from the smallest to the largest value. It is easy to point out the median of the data set when the number of values is odd. In this case, the median is automatically the middle point of the arrangement.

For example: 2, 3, 4 ,5 ,6

Using the data above, we can easily identify the median. The median there is 4. You can also prove this by using another method. After arranging the data set ascendingly, you can take the first and the last value and divide them by 2.

Solving:  [tex]\frac{2+6}{2}[/tex]= 4

15. Nine people gave contributions in pesos 100, 200, 100, 300, 300, 200, 200, 150, 100, and 100 for a door prize. what is the median contribution?


The median of the questions above is 150.

Step-by-step explanation:

The median is a middle number that found by ordering all data points and picking out the one in the middle (or if there are two middle numbers, taking the mean of those two numbers).

The median is calculated by arranging the scores in numerical order, dividing the total number of scores by two, then rounding that number up if using an odd number of scores to get the position of the median or, if using an even number of scores, by averaging the number in that position and the next position.

Is known :

There are nine value (number) = 100, 200, 100, 300, 300, 200, 200, 150, 100, and 100.

It means an odd number

How to find a median?

Step 1. Arranging scores in  numerical order.

100 100 100 100 150 200 200 200 300 300

Step 2. Devinding the total number of scores by two side

(100 100 100 100) 150 (200 200 200 300 300)

Step 3. Because the total number is odd, we can call the number in the middle as the median.

Median = 150

24. What is 300% of 200​


Answer:

600

Step-by-step explanation:

1. You divide your percentage by

100.

300 ÷ 100 = 3

2. Once you get the answer, simply multiply it by the given number.

200 × 3 = 600

-------------------------------------------

Percent means “for every 100” or "out of 100."

To Convert a Percent to a Decimal, divide a percent by 100 and remove the percent sign to convert from a percent to a decimal.

To convert a decimal to a percent, multiply the decimal by 100, then add on the % symbol.

To Find A Specific Percentage :

Convert the percentage you want into a fraction or a decimal. Then multiply the decimal or fraction by the number you want the percentage of.

Percent increase :

Work out the difference between the two numbers you are comparing.

Then divide the increase by the original number and multiply the answer by 100.

If your answer is a negative number then this is a percentage decrease.

Percent decrease :

Work work out the difference (decrease) between the two numbers you are comparing.

Divide the decrease by the original number and multiply the answer by 100.

If your answer is a negative number then this is a percentage decrease.
