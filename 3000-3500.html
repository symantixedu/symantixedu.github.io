How To Find The Mean?

The average of the numbers in a set is known as the mean. To find the value of the mean, you simply have to add the given numbers in a set and divide by how many numbers there are. In simple words, the mean is equal to the sum divided by the count.

Examples of Finding The Mean

Add the numbers: 15 + 12 + 22 + 19 + 10 + 6 = 84Since there are 6 numbers, divide the sum by 6: 84 Ã· 6 = 14The mean is 14.
