8. what is combating climate change? (need an answer at 8:30PM)


Answer:

Combating climate change refers to taking action to reduce the number of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere and prevent further global warming. Climate change is caused by the increase in greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide, methane, and nitrous oxide, which trap heat in the Earth's atmosphere and lead to a range of negative impacts, including rising sea levels, more frequent and intense natural disasters, and biodiversity loss.

To combat climate change, individuals, organizations, and governments can take a variety of actions, including:

Reducing greenhouse gas emissions: This can be achieved by transitioning to renewable energy sources, such as solar, wind, and geothermal power, increasing energy efficiency and reducing the use of fossil fuels.

Conserving natural resources: Protecting and restoring forests, wetlands, and other natural ecosystems can help to absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Promoting sustainable practices: This can include promoting sustainable agriculture, reducing food waste, and promoting low-carbon transportation options, such as biking and public transit.

Investing in research and development: Advancements in technology and innovation can help to develop new solutions for reducing greenhouse gas emissions and mitigating the impacts of climate change.

Combating climate change requires a collective effort from individuals, organizations, and governments at all levels. By taking action to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and promote sustainable practices, we can help to mitigate the impacts of climate change and protect the planet for future generations.>-<

13. work out the elapse time3.11:00am-1:30pm=​


Answer:

To work out the elapsed time between 3:00 am and 1:30 pm, you need to subtract the start time from the end time.

To do this, convert both times to a 24-hour format where 12:00 pm is represented as 12:00 and the hours beyond 12 pm are represented as 13:00, 14:00, and so on.

So, 3:00 am is 03:00 in 24-hour format, and 1:30 pm is 13:30 in 24-hour format.

Subtracting the start time from the end time:

13:30 - 03:00 = 10:30

This means the elapsed time is 10 hours and 30 minutes.

Therefore, the answer is 10 hours and 30 minutes.
