Circumference = 2 x pi x radius

If the diameter of the circle is 32 cm, the radius would be half of the diameter, which is 16 cm.

Using the formula above, we can calculate the circumference:

Circumference = 2 x pi x radius

Circumference = 100.5314 cm

Therefore, the circumference of the circle with a diameter of 32 cm is approximately 100.5314 cm.
