10. 32m - 34n - (23mn - 21n + m)​


Answer:

[tex] - 23mn + 33m - 55n[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve this equation, combine the like terms, simplfiy further, and give the answer.

Let us start solving like this:

(1) Combining the like terms

[tex]32m - 34n - (23mn - 21n + m) \\ 32 + - 34n + - 23mn + - 21 + m \\ (32m + m) + ( - 34n + - 21) + - 23mn \\ 33m + - 55n + - 23mn \\ 33m - 55n - 23mn[/tex]

(2) Further simplifying

(2a) Assigning the terms with more than one variable first than the ones with only one and assign in alphabetical order according to first variable in each term

[tex]33m - 55n - 23mn \\ - 23mn + 33m - 55n[/tex]

(3) Giving the answer

Our answer is [tex] - 23mn + 33m - 55n[/tex].

29. what is the frequency of radio waves with wavelength of 32m?​


0.094 x 10^8 = 9.4 MHz

A basic equation in wave physics is

Lf = v

The wavelength (meters) multiplied by the frequency (Hertz) is the speed (meters/second). With basic algebra this becomes

L = v/f

We are given the wavelength of 32 m and the speed of light is 3 x 10^8 m/s

(3x10^8 m/s)/(32 m) = 0.094 x 10^8 = 9.4 MHz

The FM radio stations in the USA are around 100 MHz.
