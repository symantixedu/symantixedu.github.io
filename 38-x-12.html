16. If y varies inversely as x and y = 4 when x = 9, find y when x = 12. A. 3 B. 4 C. 38 D. 36


INVERSE VARIATION

Problem:

» If y varies inversely as x and y = 4 when x = 9, find y when x = 12.

A. 3B. 4 C. 38 D. 36

Answer:[tex] \large \tt{ \color{hotpink}{y = 3}}[/tex]

_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _

Given:[tex] \sf{ y_{1}} = 4 \: \: \: \: \sf{ y_{2}} = \\ \: \: \: \: \: \sf{ x_{1}} = 9\: \: \: \: \sf{ x_{2}} =12[/tex]Step-by-step explanation:

First, write the equation of variation and find the constant (k) by substituting the values of y₁ and x₁.

[tex] \sf{y_{1} = \frac{k}{x_{1} }} \\ \sf{4 = \frac{k}{9}} \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \sf{k = 4 \: (9)} \\ \: \: \underline {\boxed{{ \sf{k = \purple{36}}}}}[/tex]

Then, solve for the value of [tex] \sf{ y_{2}} [/tex] when [tex] \sf{ x_{2} = 12}[/tex]

[tex] \: \: \: \sf{ y_{2} = \frac{36}{x_{2}} } \\ \: \: \: \sf{ y_{2} = \frac{36}{12} } \\ \underline{ \boxed{\sf{ y_{2} = \purple{3 }}}}[/tex]

Thus, the value of y is 3.
