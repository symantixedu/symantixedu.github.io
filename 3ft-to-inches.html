3. 3ft to inches what is the answer


Answer:

The answer to this problem is 36 inches.

Step-by-step explanation:

Converting one unit to another requires memorization of simple formulas and the knowledge regarding dimensional analysis or also called as Factor-Label Method.

We know that there are 12 inches in 1 foot, in mathematical terms, it is written as 12 inches:1 foot or [tex]\frac{12in}{1ft}[/tex]

Using Factor-label method

[tex]3ft * \frac{12in}{1ft} = 36 in[/tex]

20. 1.00yd=_cm(3ft=1yard)​


Answer:

1.00yd= 91.44 cm (3 ft = 1 yard)

Step-by-step explanation:

How many centimeters in a yard?

There are 91.44 centimeters (cm) in a yard. To convert yards to centimeters, multiply the yard value by 91.44.

For example, to find out how many centimeters there are in 2 yards, multiply 2 by 91.44, that makes 182.88 cm in 2 yards.

yards to centimeters formula

centimeter = yard * 91.44

What is a Yard?

Yard is an imperial and United States Customary length unit. 1 Yard = 91.44 Centimeters. The symbol is "yd".
