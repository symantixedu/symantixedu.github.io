Step-by-step explanation:

The ratio is 3:1:4

Step 1: Find the sum of all the ratio
3+1+4=8

Step 2: Divide the sum of the ratio by the combined height.
112 / 8 = 14

Step 3: Multiply the quotient by the ratio.
14 * 3 = 42
14 * 1 = 14
14 * 4 = 56

Step 4: Review your answer by adding up the product
= 42 + 14 + 56

= 56 + 56
= 112  

So the answer is 42,14,56.
