4 feet : 50 centimeters :: 1 foot : x centimeters

Solving for x, you get:

x = (50 centimeters) * (1 foot / 4 feet)

= 12.7 centimeters

So, 4 feet is equal to approximately 12.7 centimeters.

You can also use this conversion factor to convert other measurements from feet to centimeters.
