12. if one leg of 45-45-90 triangle is 11.find the length of the hypotehuse


In a 45-45-90 triangle, the two legs are of equal length. the square of the length of the hypotenuse is the sum of the squares of the other legs.

leg^2 + leg^2 = hypotenuse^2

(11)^2 + (11)^2 = hypotenuse^2

121 + 121 = hypotenuse^2

242 = hypotenuse^2

Rounding off to the 2nd decimal point the hypotenuse is 15.56
