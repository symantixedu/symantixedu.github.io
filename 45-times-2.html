23. If car travels 45 km and the time is 2 hours.Whats the answer?​


Answer & Explanation:

The answer depends on what information you are looking for.

If you want to find the speed or velocity of the car, you can use the formula:

speed = distance / time

In this case, the distance is 45 km and the time is 2 hours. So, the speed of the car is:

speed = 45 km / 2 hours = 22.5 km/hour

If you want to find out how long it would take the car to travel a certain distance at this speed, you can use the formula:

time = distance / speed

For example, if you wanted to know how long it would take the car to travel 90 km at this speed, you would calculate:

time = 90 km / 22.5 km/hour = 4 hours

So, it would take the car 4 hours to travel 90 km at a speed of 22.5 km/hour.
