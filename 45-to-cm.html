10. 4. 1 10 cm 5 cm Area = 45 cm² ?​


Answer:

This statement is not true.

The formula for the area of a rectangle is:

Area = length x width

If the length is 10 cm and the width is 5 cm, then the area of the rectangle is:

Area = 10 cm x 5 cm

Area = 50 cm²

Therefore, the area of a rectangle with a length of 10 cm and a width of 5 cm is 50 cm², not 45 cm².
