28. 45 kph to meters/second show your solution​


Problem:

45 kph to m/s

SOLUTIONS:

Long Method:

[tex] \frac{45km}{h} \times \frac{1000m}{1km} \times \frac{1h}{3600s} [/tex]

Cancel out the same units (km and h).

[tex] = \frac{45}{1} \times \frac{1000m}{1} \times \frac{1}{3600s} [/tex]

Multiply.

[tex] = \frac{45000m}{3600s} [/tex]

Simplify.

[tex] = 12.5 \frac{m}{s} [/tex]

Shortcut Method:

Simply divide it by 3.6 and change the units by m/s.

[tex] \frac{45}{3.6} = 12.5 \frac{m}{s} [/tex]

You can pick any of the two. However, if you are required to show a solution, I recommend you pick the long method.
