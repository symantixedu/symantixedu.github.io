24. The area (A) of a triangle varies jointly as the base (b) and the height (h). The area of a triangle whose height is 24 cm and whose base is 4 cm is 48 cm^2. Find the height of a triangle of whose area is 48 cm^2 and base is 6 cm.​


Answer:

Height is 16 cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

A = kbh

48 = k(4)(24)

48 = 96k (divide both sides by 96)

k = 1/2

48 = (1/2)(6)h

48 = 3h (divide both sides by 3)

h = 16

Answer:

16 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the formula for finfding the area of the triangle is 1/2 (b)(h). Then,

1/2(b)

1/2(6)= 3

Area of the triangle is 48 cm^2.

So, 48 divided by 3 is 16 cm.

The answer is 16 cm.
