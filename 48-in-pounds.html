16. A farmer harvested 48, 290 pounds of lemons from one grove and 23,710 pounds of lemons from another grove. The lemons were packed in boxes with 24 pounds of lemons in each box. How many boxes were needed to pack the lemons?​


Answer:

3,000 boxes are needed to pack the lemons.

Step-by-step explanation:

• Add the pounds of lemons harvested from the two groves.

48 290 + 23 710 = 72 000 pounds of lemons

• Divide the result to 24. Because we need to put 24 pounds of lemons in each box.

72 000 / 24 = 3 000

Therefore, we need 3,000 boxes to pack the lemons, wherein each box contains 24 pounds.
