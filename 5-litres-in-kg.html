Refugees in Nakivale

The Uganda Government General Notice No. 19 acknowledged the Nakivale Refugee Settlement as a refugee community in 1960 after it had been created in 1958.

Prepare a budget for 200 Refugees at the Nakivale Refugee Settlement Camp's monthly food expenses in 2023.

They eat meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Posho and beans are the staples of lunch and dinner. Milk and sugar are the staples of breakfast.

Given:

200 Refugees

3 Meals a day

10 patients = 3,000 every meal

5 patients = 9, 500 every breakfast

Solution:

Step 1:  Meals Budget

200 divided by 10 = 20

20 x 3000 = 60,000

60,000 x 3meals a day = 180,000

180,000 x 30 days = 5,400,000

Step 2: Milk and Sugar for Breakfast.

200 divided by 5 = 40

40 x 9, 500 = 380,000

380, 000 x 30 days = 11,400,000

Over-all total = 16,800,000

Therefore, 16,800,000 is the total budget for meals of 200 refugees in Nakivale for the month of January.
