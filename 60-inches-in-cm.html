Answer:

What are the conversions for 100 cm to inches, 60 inches to cm, and 98 yards to inches?

To convert 100 cm to inches, divide by 2.54. To convert 60 inches to cm, multiply by 2.54. To convert 98 yards to inches, multiply by 36.

So, 100 cm is equal to about 39.37 inches, 60 inches is equal to about 152.4 cm, and 98 yards is equal to about 3,528 inches.
