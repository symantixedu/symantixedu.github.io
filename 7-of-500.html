17. How many multiples of 7 are there between 200 and 500? *


Answer:

The least number between 200 and 500 that is divisible by 7 is 203.

The greatest number between 200 and 500 that is divisible by 7 is 497.

So the multiples of 7 in this range are 203, 210, 217, 224, … 490, 497.

One way to count these is to divide each by 7 to get 29, 30, 31, … 70, 71.

There are 71 - 29 + 1 = 43 values in this range.

The simplest way to do this is to count the numbers in this range, 500 - 200 + 1 = 301. And then divide this by 7, which gives 43.

If there is a remainder when dividing by 7, ignore it.
