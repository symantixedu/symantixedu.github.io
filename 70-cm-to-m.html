How to find the sq. m from sq. cm?

To find the sq m from sq cm., multiply by 10,000

Example:) 4 × 10,000 = 40,000

             So, 4 sq. m = 40,000 sq. m

In a decimal, Same as the previous, multiply by 10,000

Example:) 4.5 × 10,000 = 45,000

             So, 4.5 sq. m = 45,000 sq. m

How to find the sq. cm from sq. m?

To find the sq cm from sq m., divide by 10,000

Example:) 40,000 ÷ 10,000 = 4

             So, 40,000 sq. cm = 4 sq. m

In a decimal, Same as the previous, divide by 10,000

             So, 45,000 sq. cm = 4.5 sq. m
