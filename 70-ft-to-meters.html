Explanation:

To convert the given measurements to different units:

10 feet and 70 cm can be converted to centimeters by first converting feet to centimeters (1 foot = 30.48 cm) and then adding the additional 70 cm:

10 feet = 304.8 cm

304.8 cm + 70 cm = 374.8 cm

15 inches can be converted to centimeters by multiplying by 2.54:

15 inches = 38.1 cm

5 meters can be converted to centimeters by multiplying by 100:

5 meters = 500 cm

Therefore, the measurements in different units are:

374.8 cm

381 mm (38.1 cm x 10 mm/cm)

12.28 ft (374.8 cm / 30.48 cm/ft)

4.10 yd (374.8 cm / 91.44 cm/yd)
