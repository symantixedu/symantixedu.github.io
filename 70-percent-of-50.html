28. A senior class of 50 girls and 70 boys sponsored a dance. If 40% of the girls and 50% of the boys attended the dance, approximately what percent attended?​


Answer:

46%

Step-by-step explanation:

First, we need to find out 40% of the 50 girls. That is 50 x 0.4 = 20 girls.

Next, we need to find out 50% of the 70 boys. That is 70 x 0.5 = 35 boys.

Therefore, 20 + 35 = 55 people attended.

In total there were 50 + 70 = 120 people.

So the percent would be 55/120 = 11/24 ≈ 45.83% ≈ 46%.
