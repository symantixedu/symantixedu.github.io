5. In a circle, its area is 144π square cm. Find the radius of the circle. A. 144 cm B. 72 cm C. 12 cm D. π cm


[tex]\large\underline{\rm\color {white}{PROBLEM}:}[/tex]

In a circle, it's are is 144π square cm. Find the radius of the circle.

[tex]\sf\underline\color {white}{CHOICES:}[/tex]

A. 144 cm

B. 72 cm

C. 12 cm

D. π cm

[tex]\tt\color{red}{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline{\rm\color {white}{ANSWER}:}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline\bold\color {white}{C. \: 12 \: cm}[/tex]

[tex]\tt\color{red}{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\underline\color {white}{GIVEN:}[/tex]

[tex]\small\sf\color {white}{Area \: = \: 144π \: cm^2}[/tex]

[tex]\small\sf\color {white}{Radius \: = \: ?}[/tex]

[tex]\tt\color{red}{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline{\rm\color {white}{SOLUTION}:}[/tex]

» To find the radius, we need to take the square root of both sides so we can get the answer.

[tex]\small\sf\color {white}{ \frac{144\pi}{\pi} } = \frac{\pi {r}^{2} }{\pi} \\ [/tex]

[tex]\small\sf\color {white}{ \sqrt{ {r}^{2} } } \: = \: \sqrt{144} [/tex]

[tex]\small\sf\color {green}{R \: = \: 12cm}[/tex]

⟹ Therefore, the radius of the circle will be [tex]\small\sf\color {white}{12cm}[/tex].

23. The width of a rectangle is 12 cm. The length is twice its width. What is the perimeter of the rectangle? A. 292 cm B. 72 cm² C. 72 cm D. 292 cm²​


Answer:

C

Step-by-step explanation:

Given that the width of the rectangle is 12 cm and the length is twice its width.

Length = 2 * Width

Length = 2 * 12 cm = 24 cm

The perimeter of a rectangle is given by the formula:

Perimeter = 2 * (Length + Width)

Substituting the values, we get:

Perimeter = 2 * (24 cm + 12 cm)

Perimeter = 2 * 36 cm

Perimeter = 72 cm

Therefore, the perimeter of the rectangle is 72 cm.

29. A rectangle has a perimeter of 44 cm and area of 72 cm . Determine the length and the width of the rectangle​


Answer:

4 cm, 18cm

Step-by-step explanation:

You can solve this using mathematical expressions.

The perimeter of a rectangle equals 2L + 2W.

The area of a rectangle equals (L)(W).

All you have to do is to find L and W.

2L + 2W = 44 cm

LW = 72

2L = 44 - 2W or L = 22 - W

22W - W^2 = 72

W^2 - 22W + 72 = 0

(W-4)(W-18)

W = 4, 18

Therefore the length and the width of the rectangle are 4 and 18 cm.
