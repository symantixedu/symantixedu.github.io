7. What is the surface area of 4ft, 2ft and 7ft? with solutions please, Thank you.


Answer:

To find the surface area of a rectangular prism, we need to use the formula:

Surface Area = 2lw + 2lh + 2wh

where l, w, and h are the length, width, and height, respectively.

In this case, the dimensions of the rectangular prism are:

- Length (l) = 7 ft

- Width (w) = 2 ft

- Height (h) = 4 ft

Substituting these values into the formula, we get:

Surface Area = 2lw + 2lh + 2wh

Surface Area = 2(2)(7) + 2(4)(7) + 2(2)(4)

Surface Area = 28 + 56 + 16

Surface Area = 100

Therefore, the surface area of the rectangular prism with dimensions 7 ft x 2 ft x 4 ft is 100 square feet.

20. D=7ft. H=11 ft. find the area of the round bar?


Answer:

318.87 sq. ft.

Step-by-step explanation:

A round bar is the same as that of a cylinder shape

To determine the surface area of a cylinder, we use the formula

[tex]A = 2\pi rh + 2\pi r^{2}[/tex]

where

  r = radius

  h = height

From the given, we get that the height = 11 ft and diameter = 7 ft

A diameter is twice the radius and is expressed by the formula [tex]d = 2r[/tex]

By dividing the diameter by 2, we get the radius = 3.5 ft

By substituting the given values to the formula, we get the equation

[tex]A = 2\pi (3.5)(11) + 2\pi (3.5)^{2}[/tex]

We get the answer [tex]318.87 ft^{2}[/tex]
