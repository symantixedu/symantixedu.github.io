24. 7x -8 m and 7x + 8 m what is its area​


Answer:

49x² - 64m²

Step-by-step explanation:

Do the FOIL method in multiplying 7x - 8m and 7x + 8m.

Multiply the First terms.

7x × 7x = 49x²

Multiply the Outer terms.

7x × 8m = 56xm

Multiply the Inner terms.

-8m × 7x = -56xm

Multiply the Last terms.

-8m × 8m = -64m²

Combine like terms.

56xm + -56xm = 0

Checking:

Get the square root of 49x².

(7x

Get the square root of 64m². Copy the negative sign.

(7x - 8m)

Copy the expression then change the sign to positive.

(7x - 8m)(7x + 8m)

Therefore, the area of 7x - 8m and 7x + 8m is 49x² - 64m².
