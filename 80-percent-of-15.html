29. Find the rate1. 1 is what percents of 10?2. 10 is what percent of 20?3. What percent of 80 is 56?4.5 is what percent of 25?5.what percent of 20 is 15?Please answer..​


1) To find what percent 1 is of 10, we can use the formula:
percent = (part / whole) x 100
Here, the part is 1 and the whole is 10. So,
percent = (1 / 10) x 100 = 10%
Therefore, 1 is 10% of 10.

2) To find what percent 10 is of 20, we can use the same formula:
percent = (part / whole) x 100
Here, the part is 10 and the whole is 20. So,
percent = (10 / 20) x 100 = 50%
Therefore, 10 is 50% of 20.

3) To find what percent of 80 is 56, we can again use the same formula:
percent = (part / whole) x 100
Here, the part is 56 and the whole is 80. So,
percent = (56 / 80) x 100 = 70%
Therefore, 56 is 70% of 80.

4) 4.5 is what percent of 25?

To find the percentage, we can use the same formula:
percent = (part / whole) x 100
Here, the part is 4.5 and the whole is 25. So,
percent = (4.5 / 25) x 100 = 18%
Therefore, 4.5 is 18% of 25.

5) What percent of 20 is 15?
Again, we can use the same formula:
percent = (part / whole) x 100
Here, the part is 15 and the whole is 20. So,
percent = (15 / 20) x 100 = 75%
Therefore, 15 is 75% of 20.
