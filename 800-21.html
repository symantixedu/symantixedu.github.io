7×3=21

Answer:

We are finding the sum of the first five terms of a geometric sequence, therefore we are looking for [tex]S_{5}[/tex] and the formula to be used is the geometric series:

[tex]S_n = a_1 \cdot \frac{1 - r^n}{1-r}[/tex]

However, we still need to find the common ratio or r, and remember that this is constant between each consecutive term. Simply divide the 2nd term and 1st term:

[tex]r = \frac {a_{2}}{a_{1}}\\r = \frac {21}{7}\\\\r = 3[/tex]

With [tex]a_{1}[/tex] as 7, n as 5 and r as 3, substitute these values into the formula of geometric series:

[tex]S_n &= a_1 \cdot \frac{1 - r^n}{1-r} \\ S_{ 5 } &= 7 \cdot \frac{1 - 3^{ 5 }}{1- 3 } \\ S_{ 5 } &= 7 \cdot 121 \\ S_{ 5 } &= 847[/tex]

Therefore, the answer is A. 847.
