8. 48 is 40% of 120 21 is 25% of 84 Ano po Percentage, Base and Rate?


Answer:

1. 48 is the percentage, 120 is the base, and 40% is the rate.

2. 21 is the percentage, 84 is the base, 25% is the rate.

Step-by-step explanation:

Percentage (or portion) is the variable in the percentage formula that represents a part of the base.

The Base is the number represents 100%, or the total value of something, or the whole thing. The base is usually preceded by the word of in the given statement because it is being multiplied by the rate.

The Rate defines what part the percentage is of the base. If the rate is less than 100%, the percentage is less than the value of the base.

12. A farmer owns a total of 25 ducks and horses. The ducks and horses have 84 legs altogether. How many horses are there?


Answer:

There are 17 horses and 8 ducks.

Number of animals:

Ducks: x

Horses: y

Total: 25

Equation (1):

x + y = 25

y = 25 - x

Number of legs per animal:

Ducks: 2

Horses: 4

Total legs of 19 animals: 84

Equation (2):

2x + 4y = 84

Find the number of ducks, x.  Substitute 25-x to y in Equation (2):

2x + 4y = 84

2x + 4(25-x) = 84

2x + 100 - 4x = 84

-2x = 84 - 100

-2x/-2 = -16/-2

x = 8  ⇒  number of ducks

Find the number of horses, y:

y = 25-x

y = 25-8

y = 17  ⇒  number of horses

There are 15 horses and 8 ducks.

Check:

x + y = 25

8 + 17 = 25

2x + 4y = 84

2(8) + 4(17) = 84
