10. an isosceles triangle has a base of 20cm. The vertex angle is 80°. Find the length of each of the two equal sides of the triangle?


Answer:

15.55723827cm

Step-by-step explanation:

The sum of the interior of the triangle is 180 degrees

So 180 - 80 = 100 degrees left. Since the triangle is isosceles (2 same length) The angle left inside would be shared( ÷2 )

100 ÷ 2 = 50 degrees

50, 50, 80 degrees ----these are the angles inside. Let x be the side of the triangle.

Using the sine law.

Side1/ sin(its vertex angle) =Side2/ sin(its vertex angle)

x/ sin(50) = 20/ sin(80)

x= 20sin(50)/ sin(80)

x= 15.55723827

17. what is the volume length width height of 20cm 25cm and 32cm.​


Answer:

The volume of a rectangular prism is calculated using the formula:

V = lwh

where l is the length, w is the width, and h is the height.

Using the given measurements, we can calculate the volume of the rectangular prisms as follows:

For a rectangular prism with a length of 20 cm, a width of 25 cm, and a height of 32 cm:

V = 20cm * 25cm * 32cm

V = 10000cm^3

So, the volume of the rectangular prism is 10000 cubic centimeters.
