True, because ecosystem is any place where living things live while continuously interacting with other living and nonliving components.

TRUE 8 The living components of the ecosystem are called the biotic factors.

True, because living are called the biotic components or factors which means, (buhay na organismo).

TRUE 9. The nonliving components are called the abiotic factors.

True, because nonliving are called the abiotic components or factors which means, (hindi o walang buhay na organismo).
