Answer:

1. During sunny weather, community activities may include outdoor sports, picnics, beach trips, gardening, and outdoor festivals. On the other hand, during rainy or stormy weather, community activities may include indoor events such as movie screenings, game nights, cooking sessions, and art workshops. There may also be emergency response and preparedness activities in case of flooding or other natural disasters.

2. The activities done during sunny weather may not always be possible during rainy or stormy weather due to safety concerns and practical limitations. For example, outdoor sports may be cancelled or postponed due to slippery fields or poor visibility, and beach trips may be dangerous due to strong waves and currents. However, some activities can still be done indoors or adapted to the weather conditions, such as holding an indoor sports tournament, cooking or baking together, or attending indoor festivals and exhibitions. Emergency response and preparedness activities may also be necessary during stormy weather to ensure the safety of the community.
