24. Why does Ilocos Sur and Ilocos Norte has a windy weather?


Answer:

Windy Weather of Ilocos Norte and Ilocos Sur

The reason why Ilocos Norte and Ilocos Sur has a windy weather is because they are both facing the West Philippine Sea. The sea produces the breeze that reaches the land, making these provinces windy.

Explanation:

Because of the windy weather in Ilocos Norte, the government decided to build a wind farm that will convert the strong wind into energy. They erected numerous windmills facing the ocean, and today, the people of Ilocos Norte are benefiting a lot from these structures because aside from being a cheap energy source, it also attracts tourists from all over the Philippines. Today, more provinces around the country build their own wind farms, but the one in Ilocos Norte will always be the first one in the country.

30. A corn plant is experiencing a period of hot, dry, windy weather. How will this weather affect the corn plant processes necessary for survival?


If this kind of weather continues, survival for the corn plant is in danger, unless it is properly irrigated.  Hot and dry plus windy weather is considered to be extreme for most corn farmers.  Windy weather can makes the soil dry faster which in turn dehydrates most plants like corn.  Added to the situation is hot and dry, so basically this would damage the corn plant.  Windy weather is oftentimes the reason for insect pests population to migrate faster. This will add more adverse effects on the scenario.
