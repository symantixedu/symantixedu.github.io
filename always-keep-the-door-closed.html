20. why are you always told to close the door when inside an air conditioned room?what is its connection to energy consumption


Answer:

Why?

Explanation:

You are typically told to close the door when inside an air-conditioned room to prevent the cool air from escaping and the warm air from outside from entering the room. This is important because the air conditioner needs to work harder to cool the room if there is a constant flow of warm air entering the room or cool air escaping from the room.
