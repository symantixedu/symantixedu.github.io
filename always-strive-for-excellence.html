21. I will also empower my co-learners to strive for excellence by?


By providing various motivations and avoiding destroying the spirit of our co-learners, also focus on yourself, make some achievement targets, expand the association with smart people and always be a person who can be trust to discuss various problems while studying.

Explanation:

If we have co-learners there are several things we can do to give some empower for excellence results :

The first, is to provide motivation and confidence that our co-learners can get what is targeted and nothing is impossible to achieve. Moral support is very important and as much as possible avoid words that are negative and demoralize our co-learners.The second one, is to focus on getting good results for ourselves first, if words and advice are not strong enough to lift the spirits of our co-learners. Then prove that we can and he should too. As a friend, if he has the spirit and fighting power he will be more motivated to see what we do and the success we have achieved. That will make him struggle even more to be able to achieve maximum results.Third, make a common target to be achieved as additional motivation, for example, if we manage to get excellent results, we will celebrate it with a short vacation or a small party. Reward will affect a person's psychology to be able to strive harder.Furthermore, we can expand the learning environment with people who are smarter and have a higher enthusiasm for learning. the development of a positive environment will make learning performance better and will have an impact on better results.And lastly, being a friend who always discusses things and difficulties experienced in learning. Sometimes some people don't have a way out of the problems they face while studying so that it has an impact on the decline in learning motivation or shifting focus on the goals that should be achieved.
