3. Ano ang innovation strategy?​


Answer:

An innovation strategy is a plan used by a company to encourage advancements in technology or services, usually by investing money in research and development activities.

An innovation strategy is essential for companies that want to gain competitive advantage. An effective innovation strategy should be inspiring and add something unique to the product or service being developed. As a company, you want to increase the value of a current product or create something brand new that will draw the consumer in.

Innovation should push boundaries and be out of the ordinary. When thinking about innovation, it's impossible not to think about Apple. Apple has created and continues to create unique products with tremendous success. The iPhone, iPad, and iWatch have all been innovative products. Although smaller companies may not have Apple's global success, the beauty of innovation is that you never know what might happen with a product and the success it can bring your company.

20. ano kaya pwede ipresent sa product innovation​


Answer:

The most successful product innovations start with comprehensive market research. This can involve analysing customer surveys and focus groups, studying the competition and researching the changing needs of the target audience. Companies must also consider their own capabilities and resources, in order to capitalise on the opportunities they have found. It is crucial to ensure the product or service provides something that the customer wants, but can’t find from a competitor. Additionally, the company should know how the product or service will be marketed, where it will be sold and the strategies for success. Finally, the product development must take into account the costs to the business, and the potential return on investment when creating the product or service.

The most effective product innovations are ones that consider the needs and interests of the target audience. They contain innovative features and capabilities that persuade customers to buy the product or service. Novelty is key here, as well as user-friendliness and convenience. Customer experience should be a priority and companies should also ensure their products are reliable and durable.

Innovation should also be encouraged within the company in order to ensure the product or service is continually improved. Some companies have set up Innovation Labs where employees are encouraged to put forward ideas and collaborate on new solutions. Employee engagement and feedback should also be considered, in order to create a product or service that meets or even surpasses the customer’s expectations

Explanation:

Product innovation is the creation of new or improved products as a means of achieving competitive advantage. It enables organizations to remain competitive in the marketplace and tap into new or underserved customer needs. This can be done through either rapid product development or incremental design improvements.

Product innovation is a continuous process that involves a variety of stakeholders, including customers, suppliers, developers, marketers, investors, and industry experts. To effectively present product innovation to a broad audience, it is important to create an effective communication plan that utilizes a variety of media, including visual aids, analysis, and storytelling. Such a plan should include:

1. Establish Goals: To effectively present product innovation, it is important to begin by understanding what you hope to accomplish. Are you introducing a new product or enhancing an existing one? Do you want to increase customer loyalty or gain market share? Knowing your objectives will help you focus your messaging.

2. Reach Your Audience: It is important to determine who you are targeting with your product presentation. Are you trying to reach potential customers, existing customers, key influencers, or industry leaders? Understanding your audience will help you tailor your messaging and make sure it resonates with the right people.

3. Visual Aids: Visual aids can be an effective way to bring the product to life and engage your audience. Use images and videos to help explain the product and its features. Be sure to make the visuals memorable and creative.

4. Analysis: Utilize data and analysis to help validate the product and demonstrate why it is worth investing in. Share key metrics such as market size, customer feedback, and customer engagement.

5. Storytelling: A product presentation needs to be more than just facts and figures. It also needs to be inspiring. Use storytelling to bring the product to life and show how it can help customers achieve their goals.

6. Involvement of Experts: Involving experts such as product developers, marketers, and industry leaders can help to further legitimize the product and provide additional credibility. This can help to engage the audience and demonstrate a commitment to the product from the organization.

Product innovation is an integral part of any organization’s growth strategy. To effectively present product innovation to a broad audience, it is essential to have an effective communication plan that utilizes a variety of media, including visual aids, analysis, and storytelling. Doing so will help to engage the audience and demonstrate the value of the product.

22. ano ang kahukugan ng Prison innovation and reform​


Answer:

Prison reform is the attempt to improve conditions inside prisons, improve the effectiveness of a penal system, or implement alternatives to incarceration.[1][2] It also focuses on ensuring the reinstatement of those whose lives are impacted by crimes.

In modern times the idea of making living spaces safe and clean has spread from the civilian population to include prisons, on ethical grounds which honor that unsafe and unsanitary prisons violate constitutional (law) prohibitions against cruel and unusual punishment. In recent times prison reform ideas include greater access to legal counsel and family, conjugal visits, proactive security against violence, and implementing house arrest with assistive technology.
