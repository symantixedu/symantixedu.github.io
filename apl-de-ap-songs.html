4. PRATITIVE PROPORTION A basket of lola celing has 48 aples,kim,erika and shan share the aples in the ratio 1:1:2 how many aples will each one of them get.


Let x = no. of apples kim and erika have

2x = no. of apples shan have

Solution:

x + x + 2x = 48

4x = 48

4x/4 = 48/4

x = 12

Division of apples:

Kim and erika = x = 12 apples each

Shan = 2x = 2(12) = 24 apples
