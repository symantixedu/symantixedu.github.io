9. the apple in the table is _____________energy


Answer:

Gravitational Potential Energy

Explanation:

When an apple lays on the table, it has gravitational potential energy. As soon as it begins to move, the stored potential energy is converted into kinetic energy.

19. An apple is at rest on top of a table explanation​


Answer:

An apple sits at rest on a horizontal table top. The gravitational force on the apple (its weight) is one half of an action-reaction pair. The weight of the apple is equal to the magnitude of the upward force that the table top exerts on the apple.
