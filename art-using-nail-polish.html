23. functions or uses of the following materials used in manicure, pedicure hand spa and foot spa: 1.Assorted nail polish 2.Nail polish base coat 3.Cotton 4.Cuticle oil 5.Cuticle remover 6.Acetone nail polish remover 7.Nail polish remover 8.Cuticle cream 9.Nail polish thinner


Answer:

1. Its purpose is to strengthen nails, restore moisture to the nail, and help polish adhere to the nail.

2. A base coat helps the polish stick to the nail more easily; creates a barrier that protects nails from the damaging effects of the polish; prevents staining and peeling; and gives nails a more even texture.

3. Cotton fibers can be used in a wide variety of fabrics ranging from lightweight voiles and laces to heavy sailcloths and thick-piled velveteens.

4. It acts as an extra, last-minutes barrier to protect your nail polish.

5. The solvent that removes dead tissue surrounding your nail, leaving it soft and easy to manipulate, cuticle remover contains acid at low pH levels that dissolve dead skin. It preps your nails for a successful manicure, keeping them neat and tidy.

6. Acetone removes polish by quickly breaking apart the nail varnish and stripping the polish from the nail plate surface.

7. Cleaning Surfaces. Nail polish remover can be used effectively cleaning a range of surfaces.

8. Cuticle cream is used for moisturizing and softening the layers of dead skin surrounding the fingernails and toenails.

9. It restores thickened nail enamel to its original consistency and helps prolong the life of the nail polish.
