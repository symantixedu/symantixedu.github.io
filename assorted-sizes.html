10. A garment store owner bought assorted shirts. 45% of them were large, 20% percent of them were medium, and the rest were small size. If there was 420 pieces of small size, how many shirts were there?


Answer:

1200 shirts

Given:

Large = 45%

Medium = 20%

Small = 420 pieces of shirts

x = total number of shirts

Solution:

L + M + S = x

(.45x) + (.20x) + 420 = x

0.65x + 420 = x

420 = x - 0.65x

420 = 0.35x

420/0.35 = x

x = 1200

therefore, there are 1200 assorted shirts bought.
