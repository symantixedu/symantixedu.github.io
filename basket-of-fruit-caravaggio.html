9. A basket contains 9 mangoes, 15 bananas and no other fruit. If 3 more bananas are added to the basket, what fraction of the fruit in the basket will be bananas? ​


Answer:

Bananas will be 2/3 of the new total number of fruits in the basket.

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Original number of fruits:

mangoes = 9bananas = 15total =  (9 + 15) = 24

New number of fruits when 3 more bananas are added:

mangoes = 9bananas = (15 + 3) = 18total = (9 + 18) = 27

Fraction of bananas against new total number of fruits:

18/27

Reduce to lowest term by dividing both numerator and denominator by their greatest common factor 9:

(18 ÷ 9) / (27 ÷ 9) = 2/3

Bananas will be 2/3 of the new total number of fruits in the basket.

21. the ratio of baskets fruits in a fruit stall 1:20.if there are 25 baskets hoe many fruits are there


Answer:

There are 500 fruits in the basket.

Further Explanation

Proportion simply means equal ratio. Furthermore, a ratio can be rewritten in so many ways but the value stays the same.

Proportion has two parts, the means and the extremes. The means are the values in the inner part and the extremes are the values in the outer part. The product of two means is equal to the product of two extremes.

Example:

                            [tex]\begin{aligned}a : b &= c : d\\\\\text{extreme : mean}&=\text{mean : extreme}\end{aligned}[/tex]

This means that a and d are the extremes, and b and c are the means. The equation below is true in the concept of proportions.

                       

                                                [tex]ad=bc[/tex]

Steps in Solving the Number of Fruits

1. Let [tex]x[/tex] be the number of fruits inside the basket.

2. Construct the proportion equation

3. Equate the product of the means to the product of the extremes.

4. Apply the inverse operation to solve for [tex]x[/tex].

Solution:

[tex]\begin{aligned}1:20&=25 : x\\1(x)&=20(25)\\x&=500\end{aligned}[/tex]

This means that the number of fruits inside the basket is 500 fruits.

30. the ratio of baskets to fruits in a fruit stall is 1:20 ,if there are 25 baskets,how many fruits are there​


Ratio:

baskets : fruits

1 : 20

25 : x

Since we don't know the number of fruits yet, we will use the variable x to represent the missing number.

In order to solve this, we have to make an equation equating the given quantity and the other ratio with the variable.

1 : 20 = 25 : x

Next, we will check what has complete value, if it is the means or the extremes. The means are the ones inside the equation or the two quantitites beside the equal sign while the extremes are the ones outside. The means are complete so we will multiply it, then divide by the other quantity with no pair (20 × 25 ÷ 1). It will give us an answer of 500. Therefore, the number of fruits are 500.

The proportion would be 1 : 20 = 25 : 500
