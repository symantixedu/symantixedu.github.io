DETALYADONG DISENYO NG BATAAN-CAVITE INTERLINK BRIDGE, 34% COMPLETE NA Matapos pirmahan ng DPWH ang kontrata noong Oktubre 2020, ibinalita nito na may progreso na ang detalyadong engineering design works ng Bataan-Cavite Interlink Bridge (BCIB) Sa unang inter-agency steering committee na ginanap kamakailan, inanunsyo na 34% na ang kumpleto sa disenyo para sa flagship infrastructure project noong Pebrero 2022. Ang Asian Development Bank (ADB) country director na si Kelly Bird ay muling pinagtibay ang buong pangako ng ADB sa iba't ibang proyekto ng DPWH, kabilang ang BCIB. Nagpahayag siya ng optimismo na ang proyekto ay magiging puspusan na sa lalong madaling panahon. Binigyang-diin ng gobernador ng Bataan na si Albert Garcia ang kahalagahan ng proyekto, dahil nakatakdang ikonekta ang mga Rehiyon III at IV-A at, sa huli, ang NCR—na kanyang nakikita bilang "tatlo sa pinakamabilis na lumalagong rehiyon sa Pilipinas." Ang buong BCIB ay nagkakahalaga ng P175.7 bilyon sa pagpapatayo. Ang tulay na may apat na linya ay aabot ng 32.15km. Magsisimula ito sa Barangay Alas-asin sa Mariveles, Bataan, tatawid sa Manila Bay, at magtatapos sa Barangay Timalan sa Naic, Cavite. Kapag natapos na ang tulay, makakapagmaneho na umano ang mga motorista mula Cavite hanggang Bataan sa loob lamang ng 20-30 minuto. 7. why are meiosis in gametogenesis always interlinked?​


Answer:

The process of gamete production is gametogenesis while reductional division wherein chromosomes are halved is meiosis. For gametes to produce haploid cells, meiosis must take place. With two successive meiotic cycles, the chromosome number is reduced to half. This is why both gametogenesis and meiosis are interlinked.

25. How is culture and society interlinked with one another?


Culture and society are interlinked with one another because society is a group of people who share common things with each other, specifically culture. Culture creates an unbreakable bond among society since society tends to react to some things in similar ways based on their culture.

Further explanation :

Society is a group of people that live in the same area and share common things, such as tradition, and interests. They tend to interact with each other in a way that they are familiar with. While culture is a common thing that people in the group often share, such as the habitual actions, behaviours, and the way they think. Culture plays an important role in the making or formation of a society, because once you become a society, then you will share common things with each other.

Society and culture will always be interlinked. Because culture has an effect on a society where it affects the reaction of the people whenever there are changes or new things. Another reason is that society tends to live with each other and they eventually will create such a unique way and make it their characteristic. Different groups of people or societies will have different characteristics in life.

Therefore, when there is a group of people or society, we can see their culture just by looking and talking with them because of their ways to talk and how they react to something.. It shows that society and culture can never be separated.

27. a network of crossing and interlinked food chains is called


Answer:

A network of many food chains is called a food web. A food web consists of all the food chains in a single ecosystem. Each living thing in an ecosystem is part of multiple food chains. Each food chain is one possible path that energy and nutrients may take as they move through the ecosystem.
