You may also refer in the expiration dates and label of the products. Example chart below: 1. Tocino 2. Longanisa 3. Corned beef 4. Beef Tapa 5. Meat Loaf 6. Hotdog 7. Ham 8. Bacon 9. Ground beef 10. Pork chopsSTORING TEAMPERATURESHELF LIFE​


Answer:

Explanation:

1. Tocino

Storing Temperature:  -18 degrees C

Shelf-Life: 6 months for pillow packed, 9 months for vacuum-packed

2. Longanisa

Storing Temperature: one to two months

Fr/33//zing keeps food safe indefinitely. Keep sausages in the fr//33/zer (0 °F or less) for one to two months for best quality

Shelf-Life: -18-degree celsius

3. Corned beef

Storing Temperature: 6 degree Celsius

Shelf-Life: Uncooked corned beef in a pouch with pickling juices which has a "sell-by" date or no date may be stored 5 to 7 days

4. Beef Tapa

Storing Temperature: -2 degree Celsius

Shelf-Life: 3 days (fridge) 6 months (fr//33//zer)

5. Meat Loaf

Storing Temperature:  -18 C

Shelf-Life: 3-4 days (fridge) 3-4 months (Fr/33//zer)

6. Hotdog

Storing Temperature: -18 Degrees Celsius

Shelf-Life: 2 weeks

If there is no product date, hot dogs can be safely stored in the unopened package for 2 weeks in the refrigerator; once opened, only 1 week. For maximum quality,Fr/33//ze hot dogs no longer than 1 or 2 months.

7. Ham

Storing Temperature: 4.4 degree Celsius

Shelf-Life: Properly stored, whole ham or half ham will typically last for 5 to 7 days, or the date on the package, in the fridge. Unopened whole ham or half ham may be kept in its original store packaging when refrigerating; to maximize the shelf life of whole ham or half ham, do not open the package until ready to use.

8. Bacon

Storing Temperature: Take the bacon home immediately and refrigerate it at 40 °F or below

Shelf-Life: 2 weeks (refrigerator) 8 months (Fr/33//zer)

9. Ground beef

Storing Temperature: store ground beef at 40 °F (4.4 °C) or below and use within 2 days, orFr/33//ze. To destroy harmful bacteria, cook ground beef to a safe minimum internal temperature of 160 °F (71.1 °C).

Shelf-Life: 3 days

10. Pork chops

Storing Temperature: -5 degree celsius

Shelf-Life: 3-5 days
