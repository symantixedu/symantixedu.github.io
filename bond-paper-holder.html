13. A cylindrical pencil holder has a radius of 8cm and height of 12cm. How much paper is needed to cover the curved face of a cylinder?


Answer:

Volume Word Problems

1. The volume of the cylindrical pencil

holder is 602.88 cm³. 2. The spherical tank can hold 904.32 cubic meters of water.

Volume refers to the three-dimensional space enclosed by a boundary or occupied by an object. The volume of an object determines the amount required to fill that object. It can be measured in cubic units.

Solution:

1. The problem is asking for the volume of the cylindrical pencil holder. To find the answer, use the formula in finding the volume of a cylinder which is V = nr²h. Since the diameter is given which is 8 cm, get the half of it as the radius.

V = πr²h

= (3.14) (4 cm)² (12 cm) = (3.14) (16 cm²) (12 cm)

= 602.88 cm³

2. The problem is asking for amount of water that the spherical tank can hold. To find the answer, use the formula in finding the volume of a sphere. It is V = 4/3Tr³.

V = 4/3TTr³

= 4/3 (3.14) (6 m)³

= 4/3 (3.14) (216 m³)

= 4/3 (678.24 m³)

= 904.32 m³

21. what is the difference between stock holder and share holder?​


Answer:The rights of a stockholder or shareholder are the same, which are to vote for directors, be issued dividends, and be issued a share of any residual assets upon liquidation of a company. There is also a right to sell any shares owned, but this assumes the presence of a buyer, which can be difficult when the market is minimal or the shares are restricted. Also, a stockholder or shareholder can be either an individual or a business entity, such as another corporation or a trust.
