information). Such collections are usually printed as books, but some are now designed for

use on computers. Dictionaries have a guide word

page indicating the first or last word entry on that page.

We use a dictionary to:

>  find out the meaning of unfamiliar words

>  look for the origin of words

>  find out what type a word is (noun, verb, etc.)

A Thesaurus is a reference book with an organized list of words, their synonyms

(same meanings), and their antonyms (opposite meanings).

We use a thesaurus to:

>  find the synonyms of a word
