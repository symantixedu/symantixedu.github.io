25. what is the relationship between the broadcast signal frequency and the location of the broadcast station?​


Answer:

stations broadcast at lower frequencies, and these waves tend to go around things like buildings and hills better. FM stations transmit at higher frequencies (for higher fidelity) but these waves are more line-of-sight, which means they are easily blocked by buildings and hills.
