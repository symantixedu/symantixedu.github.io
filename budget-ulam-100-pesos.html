24. John and Mary are planning a wedding reception at Hotel Fina. It costs them a base fee of 100, 000 pesos plus 2, 500 per guest. If their budget is limited to 50, 000 pesosa. State the cost as a function of number of guests.b. Determine the expression for the inverse. c. State the domain and range for this situation.​


WEDDING RECEPTION AT HOTEL FINA

Situation: John and Mary are planning to have a wedding reception at Hotel Fina and the base fee is Php 100,000 plus Php 2,500 per guest, and their budget is limited to only Php 50,000.

a. The cost as a function of the number of guests: f(x)=100,000+2,500(x)

b. The expression for the inverse: [tex]f(x)^{-1[/tex]= [tex]\frac{1}{100,000+2,500x}[/tex]

c. The domain and range of the situation: The domain of the function is the number of guests and the range is 20, given that their budget is only 50,000.
