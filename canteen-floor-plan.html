29. the floor plan of a school canteen is in the shape of a heptagon. Four of the measures a 135°. The other angles have equal measures. what is the measure of each of the remaining angles?A. 115° B. 85° C. 270° D. 135°​


Given

The floor angle's measure of heptagon is 135°

To Find

Measure of the remaining angles

Solution

Sum of interior angles of heptagon is 900°

135 x 4 + 3x = 900°

3x = 900° - 540° = 360° = x 360° ÷ 3 = 120°

Answer

Therefore, the measure of the remaining angles of the heptagon is 120°

There is no 120° on the choices

Possible answer 115°
