Answer:

The story shows that the family is working together to prepare gifts for the children in the Bethany House. They are doing this to give back the blessings they received throughout the year. This demonstrates the value of sharing blessings and helping others. The narrator also mentioned that seeing the children's smiles brings them a wonderful feeling, which shows that they find joy and fulfillment in helping others.

Furthermore, the narrator requested to celebrate their 9th birthday in Bethany House, instead of having a party for themselves. This act shows that they prioritize helping others over their own personal desires, which is a selfless act of kindness.

Overall, the story highlights the importance of sharing blessings and helping others in achieving their goals, whether it's through giving gifts or celebrating important milestones together. By doing so, we can make a positive impact in the lives of others and find fulfillment in the act of helping.
