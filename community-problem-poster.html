Project SCHOOL COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT PROJECT PLANAssessment of Community Need:The Community NeedII.Project GoalsIII.Target BeneficiariesIV.ResourcesProblem Statement:After the implementation of this community program orproject, the following objectives are expected to achieve:1.2.3.(Be sure to set SMART (specific, measurable, attainable,result-focused, and time-bound objectives.)(Describe the profile of your target participants/beneficiariesof this community project.)(These are the people, things, and organizations that canhelp you make this project successful.)Human Resources:Physical/Financial Resources:Implementation Schedule VII. Monitoring and Evaluation of the Project Community Development Project Title: Project Description: (Tell something about the outreach method, particularly on how it will meet the need of the community or solve the current problem or issue in the community.) Promotion/ Campaign Materials (How would you promote this activity to encourage other people to be art of your activity like through Social Media, posters etc.? Also, tell something about your registration process.) Community Development Program Timeline: (Schedule here your activities included in the implementation and evaluation of this program.) March April Monitoring Timeline: (Schedule here your monitoring and evaluation of the project included the findings and suggested improvement) May Findings and Suggestions for Improvement​


Answer:

Assessment of Community Need:

The current need of the community is the lack of access to educational resources due to the ongoing pandemic. With the closure of schools and limited internet connectivity, students and parents are struggling to continue with their education.

Project Goals:

1. To provide educational resources such as modules, books, and other learning materials to students and parents in the community.

2. To conduct online classes and tutorials for students who have limited access to education.

3. To organize a community library where students and parents can borrow books and other reading materials.

Target Beneficiaries:

The target beneficiaries of this project are students and parents in the community who are facing challenges in continuing their education due to lack of resources.

Resources:

Human Resources: Trained and certified teachers, community volunteers, and coordinators.

Physical/Financial Resources:

- Modules, books, and other learning materials

- Information and communication technology equipment (e.g., laptops, tablets, and internet connection)

- Rental fee for the community library

- Marketing materials for the outreach program

- Budget for teachers and volunteers' compensation

Implementation Schedule:

- Selection, recruitment, and training of teachers, volunteers, and coordinators (March - April)

- Acquisition and preparation of educational resources and ICT equipment (April - May)

- Organizing and promoting the outreach program in the community (May - June)

- Conducting online classes and tutorials for students (June - July)

- Opening of the community library (July - August)

Monitoring and Evaluation of the Project:

Monitoring Timeline:

- Regular check-ins with teachers and volunteers

- Assessment of the number of students and parents who attended the online classes and tutorials

- Evaluation of the community library's usage and the number of materials borrowed

- Assessment of the program's effectivity in addressing the community's need

Findings and Suggestions for Improvement:

Based on the monitoring and evaluation of the project, the following suggestions for improvement are recommended:

1. Increase the number of volunteers to cater to a larger number of students.

2. Allocate a budget for creating additional educational resources.

3. Expand the community library's resources to include more books and materials.

4. Conduct a survey to assess the impact of the program on the community and gather feedback for improvement.
