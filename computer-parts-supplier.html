SELLING PRICE

==================================

Problem

A computer store pays P150.00 for a jacket to its supplier. The percent of markup is 30%. Find the selling price.

Solution

Percent discount × Original amount = Discount

[tex]\tt{D = 30\% × 150.00}[/tex][tex]\tt{D = 0.3 × 150.00}[/tex][tex]\tt\pink{D = 45}[/tex]

Original amount - Discount = Sale price

[tex]\tt{S_{Price} = 150.00 - 45.00}[/tex][tex]\tt\pink{S_{Price} = 105}[/tex]

Final Answer

[tex]\therefore[/tex] the closest answer is 105.32,thus Php 105 is the selling price of the jacket.
