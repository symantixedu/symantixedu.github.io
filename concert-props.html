6. Which of the sentences is grammatically correct? There'll be through 10,000 people at the concert. There'll be within 10,000 people at the concert. There'll be between 10,000 people at the concert. There'll be up to 10,000 people at the concert.


Answer:

Option D is the grammatically correct sentence: "There'll be up to 10,000 people at the concert."

Explanation:

The sentence is correctly constructed using the preposition "up to" when referring to a maximum number.

Answer:

The sentence that is grammatically correct is: "There'll be up to 10,000 people at the concert." This sentence means that the number of people at the concert will not exceed 10,000.

The other sentences have grammatical errors.

The first sentence should be "There'll be over 10,000 people at the concert" or "There'll be more than 10,000 people at the concert."

The second sentence should be "There'll be within a group of 10,000 people at the concert," but this phrasing is not commonly used.

The third sentence should be "There'll be between 10,000 and 20,000 people at the concert," which implies that the number of people in attendance will be within that range.

22. In the concert there is a ratio of men to women that is 2:3 if the total number of people in the concert is 350. How many women are in the concert?


Answer:

There are 210 women in the concert.

Step-by-step explanation:

Partitive Proportion

Partitive proportion is a kind of proportion. It is the identification of the parts of a whole based on given ratios.

How to solve partitive proportion?

Partitive proportion can be accomplished by adding up the ratios, dividing the whole with the sum and multiplying the quotient by its parts of the ratio.

Going back to the problem above, we need to know the number of women in the concert. There are two steps to find the answer.

1. Add up the parts of the ratio.

2. Divide the total number of people in the concert by the sum.

3. Multiply the quotient by its parts.

Given:

ratio of men to women - 2:3

total number of people in the concert - 350

Solution:

Step #1

2:3

2 + 3 = 5

Step #2

350 ÷ 5 = 70

Step #3

men = 70 × 2 = 140

women = 70 × 3 = 210

Therefore, 2:3 is 140:210.

Final Answer:

There are 210 women in the concert.

Checking:

To check, cross multiply.

140:210 = 2:3

140 × 3 = 210 × 2

420 = 420 ✔
