4. find the area of a triangle when the base is 10 cm and height is 7 cm. a. 35 cm b.70cm c.35cm^2 d.70cm^2 pa tulong po need ko po ngayon


Answer:

c. 35cm^2

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]\text{Area} = \frac{1}{2}\text{base} \times \text{height}[/tex]

[tex]\text{Given:} \begin{cases} \text{base} = 10\\ \text{height} = 7\\ \end{cases}[/tex]

[tex]\text{Area} = \frac{1}{2}(10 \times 7)[/tex]

[tex]\text{Area} = \frac{1}{2}(70)[/tex]

[tex]\text{Area} = 35[/tex]

Therefore the answer is c. 35 cm^2

Do take note that a. 35 cm cannot be answer because the unit of area always have index 2.

28. a rectangular tank measuring 70cm by 70cm by 40cm is filled up to the brim with water.Water drains from the tank at a rate of 7 liters per minute. How long will it take to drain all the water in the tank?


Answer:

28 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

We can treat this as a work question in which the volume of the tank represents work while the rate of drainage is, well, the rate.

Time = Work / Rate

Time = Volume of tank / Rate of drainage

Take note that the values are not compatible as 1 cubic centimeter or cc is equivalent to 0.001 L. So, we have to include that conversion in our computation.

Substitute then the values in order to get the time needed,

[tex]\text{Time} = \frac{\text{70 cm * 70 cm * 40 cm}}{7 \frac{\text{L}}{\text{min}}}\\\\\text{Time} = \frac{\text{196,000 cm}^3}{7 \frac{\text{L}}{\text{min}}}[/tex]

Use the conversion 1 cubic centimeter = 0.001 L,

[tex]\text{Time} = \frac{(\text{196,000 cm}^3)\frac{\text{0.001L}}{\text{cm}^3}}{7\frac{\text{L}}{\text{min}}}\\\\\text{Time} = \frac{\text{196 L}}{7 \frac{\text{L}}{\text{min}}}\\\\\text{Time} = \text{28 min}[/tex]

Therefore, it would take 28 minutes to drain all the water.
