24. She baked 2 boxes of 20 chocolate chip cookies and 18 oatmeal cookies. She gave 24 cookies to her neighbor. She distributed 8 cookies among her customers. How many cookies does Marie have now? 1. Write the numerical expression for the problem _____________________ 2. Explaine the MDAS rule in the expression _______________________ Please pa sagot po


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

So Marie got 2 boxes of cookies.

1 box is chocolate chip cookies, the other is the oatmeal cookies.

There's 20 chocolate chip cookies and 18 oatmeal cookies. All in all that's 20+18=  38 cookies.

She gave 24 cookies to her neighbor. That's 38-24= 14.

She only got 14 cookies now. Then she distributed 8 cookies among her customers. That's 14-8=6.

Marie only has 6 cookies left.

This is assuming that Marie only baked 1 box of chocolate chip cookies and 1 box of the oatmeal cookies.

We only used addition and subtraction here.
