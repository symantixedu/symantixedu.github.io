29. Slogan Theme: International year of Sustainable Mountain Development​


Slogan Theme: International year of Sustainable Mountain Development​

Here are some examples of slogans with the theme of International year of sustainable mountain development:

"Mountain communities matter - let's work together for sustainable mountain development!""Protecting our mountains - for a sustainable future!""Sustainable mountain development - it's time to take action!

2018 has been declared the International year of sustainable mountain development by the United Nations. This year is dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of mountains and sustainable development in mountain regions. Mountain regions are home to one-tenth of the world’s population and provide critical ecosystem services, including freshwater for downstream users, climate regulation, carbon sequestration, and biodiversity conservation.

The International year of sustainable mountain development presents an opportunity to highlight these vulnerabilities and draw attention to the need for integrated approaches to protecting mountain environments. Despite their importance, mountains are one of the most vulnerable ecosystems on Earth due to their fragile landscapes and exposure to extreme weather conditions. Climate change exacerbates these risks, and mountain regions are already experiencing more frequent and intense natural disasters such as floods, landslides, and droughts.
