8. 1286 dm3= _____m3 A 0.1286 m3 B 1.286 m3 C 12.86 m3 D 128.6 m3


Answer:

1.286 m^3

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve this you must know the conversion factor.

10 dm = 1m

Since we need volume we both cubed the factor

(10 dm)^3 = (1 m)^3

1000 dm^3 = 1 m^3

1286 dm^3 [(1 m^3)/(1000 dm^3)]

dm^3 will cancel out

Resulting to:

1.286 m^3
