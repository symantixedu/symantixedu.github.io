9. discrimination, and why? When do you know if respect is being shown? if there is discrimination? Cite some indicators.


Explanation:

Respect is shown when:

A person listens intently to someone and he does not interrupt the person speaking, thus, waits for him to finish.

Someone does not create an irrelevant division among the group based on race, ethnicity, origin, beliefs and religion.

We use polite words in responding to a person approaching us.

Discrimination is shown when:

We isolate a person who has a different status from us.

We humiliate someone because of their gender or sexuality.

We make someone inferior of ourselves just because we believe that we are more intelligent and active.

We show dislike against someone who has different personality than ours.
