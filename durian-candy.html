25. d.A durian with 1 kg mass is on the floor. If the durian is lifted from the floor to the top of thetable which is 2 meters from the floor, how much work is done on the durian?â€‹


Answer:

19.6 J

Explanation:

Work done to lift an object of mass m a height h: W = Fgh = mgh.

Work = mgh; where g is acceleration due to gravity on Earth (9.8 m/s^2)

Given:

m = 1kg

g = 9.8 m/s^2

h = 2 m

Solution:

W = mgh

W = (1kg)(9.8 m/s^2)(2m)

W = (1kg)(19.6 m^2/s^2)

W = 19.6 kg.m^2/s^2

(1 kg.m^2/s^2 = 1 J)

W = 19.6 J
