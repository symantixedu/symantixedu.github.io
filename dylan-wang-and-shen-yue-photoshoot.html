Answer:

500 pesos were given to Dylan by his mother

Answer:

Mother gave Dylan ₱500.00 for his birthday.

Step-by-step explanation:

To find the total amount of money Dylan was given, you simply need to get the price of the t-shirt, and the amount of change the cashier gave him, then add them together.

T-shirt price: ₱125.00

Change that was given by the cashier: ₱375.00

After getting the t-shirt price and the amount of change, you now add them together.

125.00 + 375.00 = 500.00

Therefore, he was given a total of ₱500.00
