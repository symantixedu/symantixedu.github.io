9. What is the first term of geometric sequence if the sum is 111,common ratio is -111 and number of terms is 3?


Answer:

1

Step-by-step explanation:

Sum of Geometric Sequence: [tex]S_n =\frac{a_1(1-r^n)}{1-r}[/tex] where a₁ = first term, r = common ratio, and n = number of terms

Given that Sₙ = 111, r = -11, and n = 3.

Plugging in the values,

[tex]111 =\frac{a_1(1-(-11)^3)}{1-(-11)}[/tex]

[tex]111 =\frac{a_1(1-(-1331))}{12}[/tex]

[tex]1332a_1=1332[/tex]

[tex]a_1=1332/1332=1[/tex]
