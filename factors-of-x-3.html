5. gcmf/remaining factor/factored form 4x (x +3) +5 (x +3) 8y (x -2y) - (x -2y)​


Answer:

4x (x +3) +5 (x +3)  = 4x^2 + 17x + 15

Step-by-step explanation:

4x (x +3) +5 (x +3)

Distribute 4x through the parentheses (Distributive property of Multiplication)

4x^2+12x + 5 (x+3)

Distribute 5 through the parentheses (Distributive property of Multiplication)

4x^2+12x + 5x + 15

Perform operation on like terms

4x^2+12x + 5x + 15

=  4x^2 + 17x + 15
