24. Mr Gomes is flying a kit the string is 55 meters long.what angles does the string need to form with the ground so the kite is 35 meters off of the ground


Answer:

carry on learning po wag susuko

39.52°

Given:

Hypotenuse= 55 meters

Height/Opposite= 35 meters

θ= ?

Solution:

Sinθ= opposite/hypotenuse

Sinθ= 35/55

θ= arcsin(35/55)

θ= 39.5211° ≈ 39.52°
