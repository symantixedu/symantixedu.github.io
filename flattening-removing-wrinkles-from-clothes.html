18. why the back of the palm need to be used to remove wrinkles or dirt on linen?​


Answer:

Using the back of the palm to remove wrinkles or dirt on linen can be effective because the back of the palm is generally less abrasive than the fingertips and is less likely to damage the fabric. The back of the palm can also be more effective at smoothing out wrinkles because it has a larger surface area and can apply more even pressure to the fabric. Additionally, using the back of the palm can help to reduce the risk of transferring dirt or bacteria from the fingertips to the fabric. Overall, using the back of the palm can be a gentle and effective way to remove wrinkles and dirt on linen without damaging the fabric.
