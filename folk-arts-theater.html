1. ano ang kahulugan ng Folks arts theater?


Answer:

olk Arts Theater is also known as The Tanghalang Francisco Balagtas. It is one of the performance venues of the Cultural Center of the Philipines. This covered proscenium amphitheater is where entertainment such as popular concerts, musicals, and plays are usually staged. It has 8,458 seating capacity in ten sections. The theater takes its name from Francisco Baltazar also known as Francisco Balagtas, in awe to the country's greatest poet. The theater was built in 90 days and was designed by Leandro V. Locsin in 1974.

The building was officially authorized by First Lady Imelda Marcos for the Miss Universe Beauty Pageant in 1974, which was to be held in Manila in the same year.

5. Difference between japanese theater arts and chinese theater

Difference between Japanese Theater Arts and Chinese Theater

Japanese theaters like Kabuki features/shows real or even imaginary characters while in Chinese Theater like Iyan Theater portrays the ordinary lives of people and remarkable events in the past. Japanese theater actors often wear 3D masks to show people who they are supposed to be, mostly done in a Noh kind of theater. On the other hand, Chinese theater actors wear makeup with a distinct dominant color which shows the type of character they are playing. Chinese theaters don't usually use puppets, they composed of real people wearing costumes and makeups to portray certain roles.In a Japanese Theater called Kabuki, it is composed of male casts. Meaning there are no women and every role belongs to a man. Chinese theaters composes of both female and male.Japanese theaters require their actors to be talented in music, dance and acting which are also present in Chinese theater actors but there's one difference.
