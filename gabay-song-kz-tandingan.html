Answer:

She successfully made it to “Singer 2018”; a Chinese reality singing contest where famous singers compete against one another. From being a challenger, she made it as a regular contender after surprising everyone with her amazing numbers.

Her second performance moved the Chinese audience to tears (and goosebumps) with her soulful and heartfelt rendition of a Mandarin medley which translates to “The Pain You Never Knew” (aka The Hurts You Never Knew).

Moreover, the internet exploded as her videos were played everywhere; earning great reviews. She brought her own style which netizens didn’t fail to notice; drawing fellow Pinoys and other nationalities.

Her latest performance where she did “Say Something” was no different from her first two — meaning, also a bomb performance!
