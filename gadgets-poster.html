6. The cost of producing gadget by a company is given (in pesos) by the function f(x) = 1500x + 7000. What is the cost of producing 50 gadgets? 100 gadgets?


Answer:

50 gadgets cost P82000, 100 gadgets cost P157000

Step-by-step explanation:

f(x)=1500x+7000

f(50)=1500(50)+7000

f(50)=75000+7000

f(50)=82000

f(x)=1500x+7000

f(100)=1500(100)+7000

f(100)=150000+7000

f(100)=157000
