28 = 2 * 2 * 7

32 = 2 * 2 * 2 * 2 * 2

The common factors of 28 and 32 are 2 and 2, so the GCF of 28 and 32 is 2 * 2 = 4.

To find the least common multiple (LCM) of 28 and 32, we can prime factorize the numbers and multiply the highest power of each prime factor:

28 = 2 * 2 * 7

32 = 2 * 2 * 2 * 2 * 2

The LCM of 28 and 32 is 2 * 2 * 2 * 2 * 2 * 7 = 112.
