1. what are the importance of handling the luggage correctly?​


Properly handling luggage is important for the safety of passengers and the protection of their personal belongings. Mishandled luggage can cause injury to passengers or damage to the items inside. Additionally, it can cause delays or disruptions in travel schedules if luggage is lost, misplaced, or mishandled. By handling luggage correctly, airlines and transportation companies can ensure that passengers have a safe and stress-free travel experience. It also helps to maintain the reputation and credibility of these companies by providing reliable and high-quality services to their customers.
