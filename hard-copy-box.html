14. Teachers need to print a lot of learning materials for their students such as modules and activity sheets for their pupils. Miss Santos bought a box of bond papers. Suppose in the back portion of the box it says, 20 in x 18 in x 10 in. 153571 10 in COPIER HARD COPY 20 in What is the amount of space that is enclosed in the box? 18 in​


Answer:

To find the amount of space enclosed in the box, we need to multiply the three dimensions given: 20 in x 18 in x 10 in.

The formula for calculating the volume of a rectangular solid (which is what a box is) is:

Volume = length x width x height

So, substituting the given values, we get:

Volume = 20 in x 18 in x 10 in

Volume = 3600 cubic inches

Therefore, the amount of space enclosed in the box is 3600 cubic inches.

SIMPLE PAST TENSE and PAST PERFECT TENSEThe simple past tense is frequently utilized with a verb-modifying phrase that determines a period before, like yesterday, last year, (or) an hour prior.The past perfect tense, likewise called the pluperfect, is an action word tense used to discuss activities that were finished before previously.   The formula for the past perfect tense is had + [past participle]. It doesn't make any difference if the subject is particular or plural; the equation doesn't change.

1. The storm had landed on land before classes were suspended.

Past Perfect: HAD LANDED , Simple Past: SUSPENDED

2. He was very happy after he had found his watch.

Past Perfect: HAD FOUND , Simple Past: WAS

3. He had broken his eyeglasses before he told his mother.

Past Perfect: HAD BROKEN , Simple Past: TOLD

4. By the time the flood came, the farmers had harvested the rice.

Past Perfect: HAD HARVESTED , Simple Past: CAME

5. He succeeded after he had worked so hard.

Past Perfect: HAD WORKED, Simple Past: SUCCEEDED

6. Before it rained hard, the farmers had planted the rice.

Past Perfect: HAD PLANTED , Simple Past: RAINED

7. He had gone to college before he was awarded a scholarship.

Past Perfect: HAD GONE , Simple Past: AWARDED

8. After he had thought hard, he answered the teacher's question.

Past Perfect: HAD THOUGHT , Simple Past: ANSWERED

9. When the girls had made the sandwiches, the boys brought the juice.

Past Perfect: HAD MADE , Simple Past: BROUGHT

10.After we had talked for a long time, she left her phone. ​

Past Perfect: HAD TALKED , Simple Past: LEFT
