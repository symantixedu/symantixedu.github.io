To find the elapsed time between the two given times, we need to subtract the departure time from the arrival time. Let's do this for each of the given routes:

1. Manila to Cebu:

Departure time = 14:35

Arrival time = 18:15

To calculate the elapsed time, we subtract the departure time from the arrival time:

18:15 - 14:35 = 3 hours and 40 minutes

Therefore, the elapsed time for the Manila to Cebu route is 3 hours and 40 minutes.

2. Clark to Davao City:

Departure time = 13:40

Arrival time = 16:25

To calculate the elapsed time, we subtract the departure time from the arrival time:

16:25 - 13:40 = 2 hours and 45 minutes

Therefore, the elapsed time for the Clark to Davao City route is 2 hours and 45 minutes.

3. Davao to Manila:

Departure time = 16:10

Arrival time = 19:40

To calculate the elapsed time, we subtract the departure time from the arrival time:

19:40 - 16:10 = 3 hours and 30 minutes

Therefore, the elapsed time for the Davao to Manila route is 3 hours and 30 minutes.

So, the total elapsed time between the given times is:

3 hours and 40 minutes (Manila to Cebu) +

2 hours and 45 minutes (Clark to Davao City) +

3 hours and 30 minutes (Davao to Manila) =

9 hours and 55 minutes.

Therefore, the elapsed time between the given times is 9 hours and 55 minutes.

16. 5) Clark is three times as old as Louis. In five years, Clark will be twice as old as Louis. How old is each person at present?patulong ty​


Let's call Louis' current age "L" and Clark's current age "C".

From the first piece of information, we know that:

C = 3L

From the second piece of information, we know that in five years, Clark's age will be:

C + 5 = 2(L + 5)

We can substitute the first equation into the second equation:

3L + 5 = 2L + 10

Solving for L, we get:

L = 5

Now that we know Louis is 5 years old, we can substitute that value back into the first equation to find Clark's age:

C = 3(5)

C = 15

So Louis is currently 5 years old and Clark is currently 15 years old.
