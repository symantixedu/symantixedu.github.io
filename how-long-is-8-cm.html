22. the measurement of a leg of an isosceles trapezoid is 10 cm and the bases are 20 cm and 8 cm, how long is the altitude?


Answer:

The length of the altitude is 8 cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

the measurement of a leg of an isosceles trapezoid is 10 cm and the bases are 20 cm and 8 cm, how long is the altitude?

isosceles trapezoid has equal base angles and the legs are equal

use pythagorean theorem

hypotenuse = c = 10 cm

base of the triangle = b = (20 - 8)/2 = 6 cm

altitude = a

c² = a² + b²

a = √(c² - b²)

a = √(10² - 6²)

a = 8 cm

Therefore, the length of the altitude is 8 cm.

30. the perimeterof rectangle is 58 cm. its width is 8 cm. how long the is the rectangle?​


Answer:

21 cm

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]\sf Perimeter = 2(Length + Width)[/tex]

Given:

[tex]\sf Perimeter=58[/tex][tex]\sf Width = 8[/tex]

Substituting the value in the formula,

[tex]\sf 58 = 2(Length + 8)[/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf \frac{58}{2}= Length + 8[/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf 29 = Length + 8[/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf 29 - 8 = Length[/tex]

[tex]\implies \boxed{\boxed{\sf 21 = Length}}[/tex]
