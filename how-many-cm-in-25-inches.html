Explanation:

1.) Convert 10 meters into cm (1m = 100cm).

Divide.

1,000 ÷ 25 = 40 pieces of rope

1,750 = 1.75 liters

6 feet × 30.48 = 182.88cm

72 × 2.54 = 182.88cm

4.) Convert kilometers into meters (1km = 1,000m).

76 × 1,000 = 76,000m

5.) Convert inches to feet (12 in = 1 ft).

45 ÷ 12 = 3.75 feet
