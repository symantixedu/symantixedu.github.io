6. 3 ducks lay three eggs in three days. How many eggs can ducks lay in a year? Riddle to test your brain Level: Einstein Level


Answer:

If three ducks lay three eggs in three days, then we know that each duck is laying one egg per day. Therefore, the total number of eggs that three ducks can lay in one year would be:

1 egg per day x 3 ducks = 3 eggs per day

3 eggs per day x 365 days = 1,095 eggs per year

So, three ducks can lay 1,095 eggs in one year.
