5. if a watch losing 1.5 minutes for every 12 hours that go by,how many minutes will it lose in one week


Answer:

21 minutes

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve this problem we need to be familiar with the conversions of time,

[tex]\text{1 week = 7 days}\\\text{1 day = 24 hours}[/tex]

We then use this conversion values,

[tex]\frac{\text{1.5 minutes}}{\text{12 hours}} * \frac{\text{24 hours}}{\text{1 day}} * \frac{\text{7 days}}{\text{1 week}}\\= \text{1.5 * 2 * 7 minutes}\\= \text{21 minutes}[/tex]

Therefore, the watch will lose 21 minutes in a week.
