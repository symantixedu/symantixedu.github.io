27. a car that travels 60 KMS/hr will get to it's destination in 20 minutes while a car that travels 50 KMS/he will get to it's destination in how many minutes?​


Answer:

60 : 20

50 : n

[tex]60 \times 20 = 50\times n[/tex]

[tex] \frac{60 \times 20}{50} = n[/tex]

[tex]n = \frac{60 \times 20}{50} = \frac{6 \times 20}{5} = \frac{6 \times 4}{1} [/tex]

[tex]n = 24[/tex]

60km/h : 20 mins

50km/h : 24 mins
