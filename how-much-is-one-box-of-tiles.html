4. tiles are sold by the box of 10 pieces. A box of tiles costs Php480. If mr.cruz needs 547 pieces of tiles for his apartment how much will he spend?


WORD PROBLEMS

Mr. Cruz will spend Php 26,400 for 547 pieces of tiles for his apartment.

Here are the steps to solve word problems:

Read the problem carefully to find the key details.Convert the problem into mathematical terms or mathematical statements.Identify a solution method from the provided information to what we are trying to find.Solve the equationMake sure the solution satisfies the requirements of the problem by checking.

GIVEN

tiles are sold in a box of 10 pieces. A box of tiles costs Php480. If Mr. Cruz needs 547 pieces of tiles for his apartment how much will he spend?

Solution:

A box contains 10 pieces of tiles and Mr. Cruz needs 547 pieces, then Mr. Cruz needs

547/10 = 54.7 boxes

But, the number of boxes must be an integer because tiles sold by the box, so round up

55 boxes

Now we know Mr. Cruz needs to buy 55 boxes of tiles.

A box of tiles costs Php 480, then if Mr. Cruz needs to buy 55 boxes, he will spend

Total = Php 480 × the number of boxesTotal = Php 480 × 55Total = Php 26,400

Therefore, he will spend Php 26,400 for 547 pieces of tiles for his apartment.
