[tex]\underline{\mathbb{PROBLEM:}}[/tex]

A flagpole cast of a shadow of 225 cm at the same time the shadow of a person is 125 cm and tall is 70 cm long. How tall is the flagpole?

[tex]\red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{ANSWER:}}[/tex]

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

» Identify the proportion related to the person and a flagpole to the shadows they casted. Let x be the height of the flagpole.

[tex]\frac{x}{225} = \frac{70}{125} \\[/tex]

[tex]\frac{x}{225} = \frac{14}{25} \\[/tex]

[tex]x(25) = 225(14)[/tex]

[tex]25x = 3150[/tex]

[tex] \frac{ \cancel{25}x}{ \cancel{25}} = \frac{3150}{25} \\ [/tex]

[tex]x = 126[/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] The height of the flagpole is 126 centimeters.
