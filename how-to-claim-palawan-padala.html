3. How many days limit to claim money in palawan express from sender ?


There is no days to limit to claim money in Palawan Express as long as it is there for a year. The remittance may be claimed a year given that there is a charge or penalty after thirty calendar days. After thirty days, it will be subjected to a handling fee which is one percent (1 %) of the principal amount or amount sent to you. This charge will be per month. Sometimes the handling fee will be amounting to thirty pesos (30 php).

You will be able to get the money sent to you as long as you have the following:

Valid IDTransaction numberCorrect details for the remittance
