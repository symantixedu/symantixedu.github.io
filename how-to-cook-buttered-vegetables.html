2. WHAT IS THE METHOD OF COOKING USING IN BUTTERED VEGETABLES? ​


Ingredients:

4  cloves  Garlic

3  tbsp  Butter

0.75  cup  Carrot

0.75  cup  Raw Green Soybean

0.75  cup  Broccoli Florets

0.75  cup  Cauliflower

0.75  cup  Corn Grain

1  sachet  Magic Sarap 8g

12  pcs  Quail Egg

Procedure:

Step1

(5 Min)  Sauté garlic in butter until golden brown.

Step2

(5 Min) Add carrots, green beans, broccoli, cauliflower, and corn. Season with MAGGI MAGIC SARAP. Cook for 2 minutes.

Step3

(3 Min)  Add quail eggs. Transfer on a serving plate and serve immediately.

Explanation:

Explanation:

Sentence outline for cooking Peanut Butter:

I. Ingredients

A. 2 cups peanuts

B. Salt (optional)

C. Honey or sugar (optional)

II. Roasting the peanuts

A. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)

B. Spread peanuts on a baking sheet

C. Roast peanuts for 10-15 minutes

D. Stir the peanuts occasionally to ensure even roasting

III. Blending the peanuts

A. Allow the peanuts to cool for a few minutes

B. Transfer the peanuts to a food processor or blender

C. Blend the peanuts for 1-2 minutes

D. Scrape down the sides of the blender as needed

E. Continue blending until the mixture is smooth and creamy

IV. Adding optional ingredients

A. Add salt to taste (optional)

B. Add honey or sugar to sweeten (optional)

C. Blend the mixture for an additional minute

V. Storing and serving

A. Transfer the peanut butter to a jar or airtight container

B. Store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks

C. Serve as desired, such as on bread or with fruits and vegetables.
