Answer:

1. Before heating, the materials appear to be in their original forms. Liquid Sosa is a clear liquid, baking soda is a white powdery substance, toothpaste is a semi-solid with a paste-like consistency, mouthwash is a liquid, and detergent is typically a powder or liquid.
