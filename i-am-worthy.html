4. am i worthy to be a part of moving up ceremony


Answer:

Definitely, yes.

Explanation:

Every student who enters school deserves to step up and to make a gradual progress in their academics for as long as they make the necessary efforts.

If you are confident that you have met the requirements and expectations of your academics, then you are certainly deserving to be a part of the moving up ceremony in your school.  Your hard works, sacrifices, and great deal of efforts will pay off in due time and that is your moving up ceremony.

Over the years of your school life, you should have the courage to claim that you are deserving because simply you are.  You may have failed a lot of times during your journey toward your moving up, but look back and be grateful, because those failures have pushed you to reach that point where you have to be at your best.  You have met a new version of yourself and that is part of your continuous growth and progress.
