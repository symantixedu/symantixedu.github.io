Answer:

[tex]180 \textdegree=x \textdegree+46 \textdegree+76 \textdegree[/tex]

[tex]x \textdegree=180 \textdegree-46 \textdegree-76 \textdegree[/tex]

[tex]x \textdegree=58 \textdegree[/tex]
