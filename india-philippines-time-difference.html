If it is 02:00 H in Cairo, Egypt, what time is it in the Philippines?​


1. 5 hours

2. 8:15 of Friday in the Philippines

3. 23:25 of yesterday in Warsaw, Poland

4. 2:30 of Monday in the Philippines

5. 8:00 of the same day in the Philippines

How?

1. 10:15 - 05:15 = 5 hours

2. Tokyo, Japan is an hour ahead of the Philippines, therefore decreasing an hour from 09:15 to find the time of the Philippines.

3. The Philippines is 6 hours ahead of Warsaw, therefore removing 6 hours from 5:25 to find its time. But that puts us to the previous day- I mean, night, so, while following the 24-hour format, the time is 23:25 of yesterday.

4. The Philippines is 2 and a half hours ahead of New Delhi, therefore adding the said time to 1:00 to find the Philippines' time.

5. The Philippines is 6 hours ahead of Cairo, therefore adding 6 hours to 02:00 to find the Philippines' time.
