If she arrived at 2:35 pm in Dubai's time, how many hours is the time difference of the two countries?​


Answer:

4 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

Suppose this is a domestic flight, if Mrs. Villamor left 2:15 pm and it will take 4 hrs and 20 minutes for her flight, she should've arrived 6:35 pm.

We need to subtract the ideal time to that of the actual time to get the time difference.

6:35 - 2:35 = 4 Hrs.

Therefore, there is 4 hr difference between time of Philippines and Dubai.

Ang pamahalaan ay dapat magbigay ng tulong at suporta sa pamilya at mga kaanak ng biktima ng salang pagnanakaw. Maari rin silang magbigay ng financial assistance o iba pang uri ng tulong sa pamilya ng biktima. Mahalagang tiyakin na ang mga biktima ng krimen at kanilang mga pamilya ay nabibigyan ng tamang proteksyon at pagkalinga ng pamahalaan upang maibsan ang kanilang mga pinagdadaanan.
