ANSWERS:

with solutions

1. My mother gave me P1 50, my sister gave me 4 times as much. My father gave me 6 times as much as my mother. How much money do I have?

ANSWER: 3,600

SOLUTIONS: 150 × 4 = 600; 600 × 6 = 3,600

2. in the farm there are 12 ducks and 1.75 times more goats than ducks. There are also 2.5 times as more pigs than ducks. How many animals are there in the farm?

ANSWER: There are 51 animals in the farm.

SOLUTIONS: 12 × 1.75 = 21; 12 × 2.5 = 30; 21 + 30 = 51

3. Edna had 100 boxes of face mask. She sold 50 boxes on monday and half of the remaining on Tuesday. She decided to tell sell the remaining on Wednesday is resolved the face mask 150 pesos per box how much did he earn on Monday and Tuesday?

ANSWER: He earned 7,500 on Monday and 3,750 on Tuesday.

SOLUTIONS: 150 × 50 = 7,500; 50 ÷ 2 = 25; 150 × 25 = 3,750

4. every week vangie saves for the coming Christmas. On the first week of the year she saves 450.00 pesos, on the second week 500.00 pesos, on the third week. 550.00 pesos. If the pattern continues, how much is her savings on the 6th week?

ANSWER: Her money on the 6th week is 9,000

SOLUTIONS: (450 + 500 + 550) × 6 = 1,500 × 6 = 9,000

5. Jette lives in a city that has an area of 344.6 square kilometers. friend lives in a town that is one-tenthor 0.1 that size. What is the area of her friends town?

ANSWER: 34.46

SOLUTION: 344 × 0.1 = 34.46
