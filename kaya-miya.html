11. On the given ML map, the distance between Layla and Miya is describe by the expression 5x - 15 meters, and the distance between Nana and Zilong is 3x +3 meters. If Layla wants to attack Miya, how far does she needs to walk to reach Miya?​


Answer:

3x + 8 meters

Step-by-step explanation:

If the distance between Layla and Miya is given by the expression 5x - 15 meters, and Layla wants to attack Miya, then she needs to walk the entire distance between them.

So, to find the distance between Layla and Miya, we need to know the value of x. We can find x by using the distance between Nana and Zilong, which is given by the expression 3x + 3 meters.

Let's assume that Nana is located between Layla and Miya, and Zilong is located on the other side of Miya. In this case, the distance between Layla and Zilong is the sum of the distance between Layla and Nana (5x meters) and the distance between Nana and Zilong (3x + 3 meters):

Distance between Layla and Zilong = 5x + 3x + 3 = 8x + 3 meters

Since we want to find the distance between Layla and Miya, we need to subtract the distance between Miya and Zilong (which is equal to the distance between Layla and Zilong) from the distance between Layla and Miya:

Distance between Layla and Miya = Distance between Layla and Zilong - Distance between Miya and Zilong

= (8x + 3) - (5x - 15)

= 3x + 18 meters

Therefore, Layla needs to walk a distance of 3x + 18 meters to reach Miya.
