22. which knot is the simplest type of knot and is used to make a knob in a rope, string, or cord? a.bowline knot b.slip knot c.square knot d.overhand knot​


Answer:

D. overhand knot

Explanation:

The overhand knot is the simplest type of knot and is used to make a knob in a rope, string, or cord. It is used for tying packages, to keep rope ends from fraying, and as a first step in making more complex knots such as the surgeon's knot and the square knot.

24. Why is knot tying is important in the culture of the three countries? (China,Japan,Korean).


Answer:

Knot tying is important in the culture of China because knots are supposed to ward off evil spirits, hence are a symbol of longevity and eternity.

Knot tying is important in the culture of Japan because the traditional red and white cords express a life-affirming and celebratory message.

Knot tying is important in the culture of Korea because modern Korean artists are using the traditional knots in their works, such as accessories, jewelry and home interior decorations.
