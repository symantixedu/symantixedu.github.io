6. What is the communication function of the movie Kung Fu Panda Oogways Best Quotes and explain​


“Yesterday is history, tomorrow is a mystery, and today is a gift… that’s why they call it the present”

― Master Oogway

In the scene that Master Oogway gives a quote that is considered one of the best quotes in the Kung Fu Panda film. The communication function used is to do motivation or "motivate function".

Communication plays an important role in information dissemination related to any form of human activity. Communication is the process of exchanging messages, ideas, facts, opinions, or feelings. The functions of communication in an organization are to inform, persuade, and motivate.

In that quote, Master Oogway tries to convey to Po and the entire world audience who watched the Kung Fu Panda movie that life and what we have today is the greatest gift and bounty, that's why it's called "present." So what we have to do is live today and every day is doing all the positive things to be grateful for the gifts that have been given

No need to regret what has happened in the past, let it be history that we can use as lessons and self-improvement to become better. There is also no need to guess what will happen tomorrow and the future because let it be a mystery, and focus enough so that you can prepare yourself to be better when that time comes.

What is conveyed (communication) Master Oogway is a sentence that is motivating as contained in the communication function.

17. Task 8.2 A QUOTE ON QUOTE


The phrase “quote unquote” means speaking a quotation on a word for word basis. You end the quotation with the word “unquote” to indicate that it’s finished. On the one hand, the phrase “quote on quote” is a mispronunciation of “unquote.” The correct phrase is “quote unquote.” This indicates that what you’re saying is a repetition of someone else’s statement.
