14. benefit of LACK OF TECHNOLOGY IN HANESSING RENEWABLE ENERGY. ano magiging cause nya na maganda​


Answer:

One benefit of not relying heavily on technology to harness renewable energy is that it reduces the environmental impact caused by technology. By relying on natural processes, renewable energy sources can be accessed with minimal disturbance to the environment. This reduces the risk of damaging ecosystems or disrupting local wildlife populations.

Good Cause

The lack of technology in harnessing renewable energy for good causes by humans can be a major challenge. Renewable energy sources such as solar, wind and geothermal are abundant, but the current technology to harness them efficiently is still in its infancy. This means that in many cases, the cost of utilizing renewable energy sources is still prohibitively high for many people, making them largely inaccessible. Additionally, the infrastructure needed to utilize renewable energy sources on a large scale is still in its development stages, which can make it difficult for governments and organizations to invest in such projects. This lack of technology also means that there is a lack of knowledge and experience in the field, which can be a hindrance to progress.

19. "BARRIERS"Lack of TimeSocial InfluenceLack of EnergyLack of MotivationFear of InjuryLack of SkillLack of ResourcesWeather Conditions, Travel or Vacation."Ways to Overcome Barriers to Sports Participation"​


Answer:

Lack of time

Add physical activity to your daily routine. For example, walk or ride your bike to work or shopping, organize school activities around physical activity, walk the dog, take the stairs, exercise while you watch TV, park farther away from your destination, etc.

Social Influence

Invite friends and family members to exercise with you. Plan social activities involving exercise. Develop new friendships with physically active people. Join a group, such as the YMCA or a hiking club.

Lack of energy

1.Schedule physical activity for times in the day or week when you feel energetic.

2.Convince yourself that if you give it a chance, physical activity will increase your energy level; then, try it.

Lack of motivation

1. Plan ahead. Make physical activity a regular part of your daily or weekly schedule and write it on your calendar.

2. Invite a friend to exercise with you on a regular basis and write it on both your calendars.

3. Join an exercise group or class.

Fear of injury

1. Learn how to warm up and cool down to prevent injury.

2. Learn how to exercise appropriately considering your age, fitness level, skill level, and health status.

3. Choose activities you feel you can do safely, and increase the amount you do gradually as your confidence and abilities grow.

Explanation:

Lack of clarity refers to unclear or confusing communication or information that makes it difficult for people to understand what is being conveyed. An example of lack of clarity would be a written instruction manual that uses technical jargon without explaining it, making it difficult for the reader to follow the steps.

Lack of creativity refers to a lack of originality or imagination in thought or expression. An example of lack of creativity would be repeating the same ideas or solutions in a given problem, without trying to think outside of the box or coming up with new ideas.
