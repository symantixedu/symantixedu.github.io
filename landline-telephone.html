1. a type of wire used for landline telephones and internet hookups?​


Answer:

Phone and Data Wire

Explanation:

Telephone and data wiring are low-voltage wires used for “landline” telephones and internet hookups. Telephone cable may contain four or eight wires. Category 5 (Cat 5) cable, the most common type of household data wiring, contains eight wires wrapped together in four pairs.
