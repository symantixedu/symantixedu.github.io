10. When a piece of hard-boiled egg is left in contact with a silver spoon, a black spot is formed on the spoon?


Yes

This is because the small sulfur content in the egg white reacts with the iron is the spoon to form ferrous sulphide which is black in colour. This is why a black spot is observed on the spoon.

Chemical formula:

Fe + S = FeS

17. why is right hand used for spoon and left hand for fork?​


Answer:

Prior to the adoption of the fork, the custom in Europe was for all food to be conveyed to the mouth by the right hand (using a spoon, a knife, or fingers). When the fork was adopted, it followed this rule; it was held in the left hand while cutting and then transferred to the right to eat.

Answer:

Give the definition of this tools.

1. Tenderizers- A meat tenderizer, meat mallet, or meat pounder is a hand-powered tool used to tenderize slabs of meat in the preparation for cooking. Although a meat tenderizer can be made out of virtually any object, there are three types manufactured specifically for tenderizing meat.

2. Serving spoon- a large spoon or ladle used to serve out individual portions of food.

3. Kitchen Shears- Kitchen shears, also known as kitchen scissors, are intended for cutting and trimming foods such as meats. Modern scissors are often designed ergonomically with composite thermoplastic and rubber handles which enable the user to exert either a power grip or a precision grip.

4. Measuring cup- a cup marked in graded amounts, used for measuring ingredients in cooking.

5. Blenders- A blender is a kitchen and laboratory appliance used to mix, crush, purée or emulsify food and other substances. A stationary blender consists of a blender container with a rotating metal blade at the bottom, powered by an electric motor that is in the base.
