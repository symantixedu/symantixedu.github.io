11. Did mme. loisel have a reason to be unhappy after her marriage or was unhappiness a result of her character?


Mathilde Loisel has no reason to be unhappy after her marriage because her husband has a decent job and they have a good relationship. Her unhappiness stems from her character. She is unhappy because she is self-conscious about her social standing. Mathilde Loisel has always fantasized about a lavish life with servants and such, and she is dissatisfied because she is not wealthy. She believes she married beneath her and is unimpressed with their way of life.

She has physical beauty but not the affluent lifestyle she desires, and she is deeply dissatisfied with her lot in life. Her husband makes every effort to meet Mathilde's demands and soothe her complaints.
