Answer:A solar eclipse occurs when the moon comes between the earth and the sun. A lunar eclipse occurs when the earth comes between the moon and the sun. The eclipse of the sun(solar eclipse) takes place during the daytime. The eclipse of the moon(lunar eclipse) takes place during the nighttime.

What is Lunar Eclipse?

Lunar Eclipse happens when the Earth moves between the Sun and the Moon, blocking the Sun's light from reaching the Moon.

When the Moon travels to the Penumbra, we see a Penumbral Lunar Eclipse, where the Moon is faintly visible to us. When part of the Moon is in the Penumbra and a part of it is in Umbra, we see a Partial Lunar Eclipse and when the Moon travels to the Umbra, we see a Total Lunar Eclipse.

There are times when the Moon appears to be red. The colors of the rays of the Sun are red, orange, blue, green, and violet. 27. what is the difference a total lunar eclipse and a partial lunar eclipse


A lunar eclipse is divided into three types: penumbral, partial, and total. A penumbral lunar eclipse is one in which the moon just passes through the Earth’s penumbra. A partial lunar eclipse occurs when the moon passes into the Earth’s umbra region. A total lunar eclipse occurs when the moon entirely circles within the umbra region of the Earth. A total lunar eclipse combines all the three phases. The moon is not seen to be completely dark in a total lunar eclipse.

Question

What is the difference a total lunar eclipse and a partial lunar eclipse?

Answer

A partial lunar eclipse occurs when the moon passes into the Earth's umbra region. A total lunar eclipse occurs when the moon entirely circles within the umbra region of the Earth. The moon is not seen to be completely dark in a total lunar eclipse.

Total Lunar Eclipse:

A total lunar eclipse occurs when the Moon and the Sun are on exact opposite sides of Earth. Although the Moon is in Earth's shadow, some sunlight reaches the Moon. The sunlight passes through Earth's atmosphere, which filters out most of the blue light. This makes the Moon appear red to people on Earth.
