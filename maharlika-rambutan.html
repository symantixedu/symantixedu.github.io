3. 4kg of rambutan is php 200.00 the cost of 6kg of rambutan is? with solution please


Answer:

300

Step-by-step explanation:

200 is equivalent of 4kg

+ 100 that is the half of 4kg rambutan which is 2kg now let's just add them together so we can get the answer 300

200.00 ÷ 4 = 50

200.00÷ 2= 100

6-4=2 that means 2kg of rambutan is 100.00 pesos

To find the cost of 6kg of rambutan, we can use the unit rate of the given information, which is:

Cost of 1kg of rambutan = PHP 200.00 / 4 kg = PHP 50.00/kg

To find the cost of 6kg of rambutan, we can multiply the unit rate of 1kg by 6:

Cost of 6kg of rambutan = PHP 50.00/kg x 6 kg = PHP 300.00

So the cost of 6kg of rambutan is PHP 300.00

18. is Rambutan a fruit?​


Answer:

Yes, rambutan is a tropical fruit

Explanation:

The rambutan is a medium-sized tropical tree in the family Sapindaceae. The name also refers to the edible fruit produced by this tree. The rambutan is native to Southeast Asia. It is closely related to several other edible tropical fruits including the lychee, longan, pulasan and mamoncillo.
