2. The barangay will put a cylindrical drum at the back of their barangay hall with an open top whose radius and height are 12 and 32dm, respectively, What is the surface area? Show your solution​


Surface Area of a Cylinder

The surface area of any three-dimensional solid is the sum of the areas of its faces.

[tex]\sf\underline{Problem:}[/tex]

The barangay will put a cylindrical drum at the back of their barangay hall with an open top whose radius and height are 12 and 32dm, respectively, What is the surface area? Show your solution

[tex]\sf\underline{Solution:}[/tex]

To find the surface area of a cylinder, use the formula [tex] \sf{SA=2\pi r^2+2\pi rh} [/tex], where r is the radius and h is the height of the cylinder.

[tex]\sf{SA=2\pi r^2+2\pi rh}[/tex][tex]\sf{SA = 2 \times \pi \times 12dm^2 + 2 \times \pi \times 12dm \times 32dm}[/tex][tex]\sf{SA =75.36dm + 2411.52dm}[/tex][tex]\sf{SA = \green{2486.88dm^2}}[/tex]

Therefore, the surface area of the cylindrical drum is 2,486.88dm²

Ang isang barangay hall ay ang upuan ng lokal na pamahalaan para sa barangay, ang pinakamababang nahalal na dibisyon ng administratibong Pilipinas, na mas mababa sa isang lungsod ng Pilipinas o munisipalidad ng Pilipinas.
