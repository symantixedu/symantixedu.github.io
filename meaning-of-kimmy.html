3. The sum of kimmy and doras age is 53. Five years ago, kimmy was 7 years more than the half as old as dora. How old is dora?


Answer:

40 yrs old

Step-by-step explanation:

let kimmy be = x

and dora = y

x + y = 53

Five years ago means that we will subrtact 5 to their ages

x - 5 + 7 = y/2 - 5

x + 2 = y/2 - 5

Now you can either solve for x or y to substitute to the original equation.

x + 2 = y/2 - 5

x + 2 - 2 = y/2 - 5 - 2

x = y/2 - 7

Subtistute the value of x to the original equation.

x + y = 53

y/2 - 7 + y = 53

2 [y/2 - 7 + y = 53]

y - 14 + 2y = 106

3y - 14 = 106

3y - 14 + 14 = 106 + 14

3y = 120

3y/3 = 120/3

y = 40

then dora is 40

Just to be sure lets solve for Kimmy's age :

x + y = 53

x + 40 = 53

x + 40 - 40 = 53 - 40

x = 13

substitute the 2 values to the 2nd equation :

x + 2 = y/2 - 5

13 + 2 = 40/2 - 5

15 = 20 - 5

15 = 15

so Our answer is correct Dora is 40 years old

29. Kimmy worked from 7:00 a.m. to 5:15 p.m. with a 45-minute break. If Kimmy earns P120.00 per hour and does not get paid for her breaks, how much will she earn?


Answer:

P1,140.00

Step-by-step explanation:

time duration is 7:00 am to 5:15 pm, with 45 minutes break, we subtract 45 minutes to her work time, say instead of working for 10 hours and 15 minutes, she will only be working for 9 hours and 30 minutes.

120 pesos per hour, 120 times 9. we get 1,080.

since 30 minutes is half an hour, we add 60, which is half of 120, the payment per hour.

1,080 + 60 = P1,140.00
