If the function is shifted left 4 units then shifted up by 7 units and reflected over the y-axis, what is the equation of the new function?2) Given the function f(x) = √x. If the function is shifted right 4 units, then shifted down 5 units and reflected about the y-axis, what is the equation of the new function?pa answer ngani kara :(​


[tex] \large \bold{Answers:}[/tex]

g(x)=x^2 -8x +23g(x)=√(-x-4) -5

The transformations of interest are ... f(-x) . . . . reflects across the y-axis f(x -h) +k... translates by (h, k)

1.)

The translation left 4 and up 7 is translation by (-4, 7), so the translated function is ...

f(x +4) +7

Reflection over the y-axis replaces x with -x (and also reflects the left-translation). The reflected translated function is ...

g(x) = f(-x + 4) + 7 = (-x + 4)² +7 = x² - 8x + 16 + 7

g(x) = x² - 8x + 23

2.)

Translation right 4 and down 5 is translation by (4, -5), so the translated function is ...

f(x - 4) -5

Reflection over the y-axis replaces x with -x. The new equation is

g(x) = f(-x-4) -5

g(x) = √(-x - 4) -5
