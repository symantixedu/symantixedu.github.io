Listahanan, or the National Household Targeting System for Poverty Reduction (NHTS-PR), is an information management system that employs geographic targeting, household assessment, and validation in order to provide national government agencies, development partners, and other social protection actors with information.

ANSWER :

The nth term is An = 5n + 1.

STEP BY STEP EXPLANATION :

An = A1 + (n - 1) × R

An = 6 + (n - 1) × (11 - 6)

An = 6 + (n - 1) × 5

An = 6 + 5n - 5

An = 5n - 5 + 6

An = 5n + 1

Testing...

The 1th term.

An = 5n + 1

A1 = 5(1) + 1

A1 = 5 + 1

A1 = 6

The 2th term.

An = 5n + 1

A2 = 5(2) + 1

A2 = 10 + 1

A2 = 11

...

The 5th term.

A5 = 5n + 1

A5 = 5(5) + 1

A5 = 25 + 1

A5 = 26

Therefore , the nth term is An = 5n + 1.
