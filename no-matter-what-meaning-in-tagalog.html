24. what are the 4 types of matter and what is the meaning of matter?​


1. Solid Matter: Matter that has a distinct, recognizable shape and a definite volume.

2. Liquid Matter: Matter that takes the shape of its container and has a definite volume.

3. Gas Matter: Matter that has neither a definite shape nor a definite volume and expands to fill its container.

4. Plasma Matter: Matter composed of ionized atoms and molecules, which has a high degree of energy and does not take the shape of its container.

Matter is defined as anything that occupies space and has mass.
