10. 1.Given Event A = (2,4,6,8) and B=(1,2,3,4,5), Event find: a.AUB b. ANB 2. What is the probability of gotting an odd number and numbers loss than Tive in a spinning wheel chart with numbers 1-10?pahelp po plssss​


Step-by-step explanation:

a. AUB means the union of A and B, which is the set of all elements that belong to A, B, or both. So, AUB = (1,2,3,4,5,6,8).

b. ANB means the intersection of A and B, which is the set of all elements that belong to both A and B. So, ANB = (2,4).

The spinning wheel chart has 10 numbers, of which 5 are odd (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) and 5 are less than 5 (1, 2, 3, 4).

To find the probability of getting an odd number and a number less than 5, we need to find the number of outcomes that satisfy both conditions and divide by the total number of possible outcomes.

The outcomes that satisfy both conditions are 1, 3, and 4. So, the probability of getting an odd number and a number less than 5 is 3/10 or 0.3.
