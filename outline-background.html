6. Sample Outline for a Position Paper I Introduction A. Introduce the topic B. Provide background on the topic C. Assert the thesis (your view of the issue)​


Sure, here's an example outline for a position paper on the topic of climate change:

I. Introduction
A. Introduce the topic: Climate change is a pressing issue that affects the entire planet.
B. Provide background on the topic: Climate change refers to the long-term changes in the Earth's climate, including rising temperatures, changes in precipitation patterns, and more frequent severe weather events.
C. Assert the thesis: Climate change is a serious problem that requires immediate action to mitigate its effects and prevent further damage to the planet.

II. Background Information
A. Provide more detailed background information on the topic: Climate change is caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation, which release large amounts of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere.
B. Present different perspectives on the issue: Some argue that climate change is a natural phenomenon that is not caused by human activity, while others believe that the issue is exaggerated or overstated.

III. Main Argument
A. Present your main argument in support of your thesis: Climate change is a real and pressing issue that requires immediate action to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and transition to renewable energy sources.
B. Respond to counterarguments: Address the argument that climate change is not caused by human activity by providing evidence of the correlation between rising temperatures and human activity. Address the argument that the issue is exaggerated by providing evidence of the impacts of climate change, such as rising sea levels and more frequent severe weather events.

IV. Conclusion
A. Summarize your main points: Climate change is a serious problem that is caused by human activity and requires immediate action to mitigate its effects.
B. Emphasize the importance of your position: Climate change has far-reaching impacts on the planet and its inhabitants, and it is necessary to take action to prevent further damage.
C. Call to action: Encourage readers to take action to reduce their carbon footprint, support renewable energy sources, and advocate for policies that address climate change.

RESEARCH TOPIC/TITLE: The Impact of Social Media on Consumer Behavior among ABM Students

RESEARCH QUESTION: How does the use of social media influence the buying behavior of ABM students in the Philippines?

BACKGROUND OF THE STUDY:

Why is it necessary to conduct a study about the topic?

The use of social media has become increasingly prevalent in today's society, and it has had a significant impact on consumer behavior. ABM students are a vital demographic for businesses as they represent the future of the workforce and are expected to have higher purchasing power. Therefore, it is essential to study how social media affects their buying behavior to help businesses better understand their target market.

What is the basis problem, issue, or situation for conducting a study about the topic?

Social media has changed the way people interact with each other and has also transformed the way businesses communicate with consumers. With the rise of social media platforms, there is an increasing concern about its impact on consumer behavior. Many businesses are struggling to understand how social media influences their target market's buying decisions, including ABM students, who are expected to have significant purchasing power.

What is the context or background of the topic?

Social media has become an integral part of the daily lives of ABM students. They use social media platforms to connect with their peers, consume content, and make buying decisions. As a result, businesses are increasingly using social media platforms to promote their products and services. This study aims to investigate the impact of social media on the buying behavior of ABM students and help businesses gain a better understanding of how to market their products and services to this demographic.

Answer:

When introducing a topic in an organized manner, it's important to provide context, identify the problem or gap in knowledge, and highlight the significance of the research. This can be done by providing background information, outlining the research questions or objectives, and previewing the main points to be covered.

The background of the study serves as the foundation of the research by providing context and establishing the rationale for the study. It helps to identify the research problem, highlight the gaps in existing knowledge, and explain why the study is important. The background also provides a framework for the research by identifying the key concepts, theories, and research methods that will be used.

The outline can be a useful tool for organizing the background of the study by providing a structure for the information to be presented. It can help to ensure that all relevant information is included and that the information is presented in a logical and coherent manner. The outline can also help to identify any gaps or areas where additional research may be needed.
