-A blouse is a loose-fitting upper garment that was worn by workmen, peasants, artists, women, and children. It is typically gathered at the waist or hips (by tight hem, pleats, parter, or belt) so that it hangs loosely ("blouses) over the wearer's body. 13. The pants cost twice as much as the blouse. If the amount of pants and blouse is 900. How much is the cost of the blouse?​


Let's use algebra to solve the problem.

Let "x" be the cost of the blouse, in dollars. Since the pants cost twice as much as the blouse, the cost of pants is 2x.

According to the problem, the total cost of a blouse and pants is $900:

x + 2x = 900

Simplifying the equation, we get:

3x = 900

Dividing both sides by 3, we get:

x = 300

Therefore, the cost of the blouse is $300. And the cost of the pants is 2x = $600.
