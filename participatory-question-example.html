Answer:

PARTICIPATORY APPROACH DIFF.

A participatory approach means that the person in charge of solving a problem or designing an innovation involves people who are directly concerned by the result ofhis or her work.

PARTICIPATORY PLANNING APPROACH DIFF.

Participatory planning is an approach to designing active, livable cities, which makes urban planning accessible, community-driven, and fun. It is grounded in the belief that blending local knowledge and expert knowledge leads to strong outcomes.

9. Participatory governance defination


As the name implies, participatory governance strengthens its management by encouraging participation from the citizens. Since citizens form part of the nation, it is their right to be aware of what is going on in terms of policies that concern them. As the world becomes more innovative and educated, the citizens are well-equipped to share their perspective and experiences through dialogues and townhall meetings. Societies are fighting for human rights and democracy. One seemly way of practicing this is by allowing citizens to engage in participatory governance.
