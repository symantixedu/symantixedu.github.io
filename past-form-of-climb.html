13. What form of energy is demonstrated in climbing staircases?​


Answer:

Potential Energy

Explanation:

By climbing the stairs, your body converted kinetic energy to potential energy. Potential energy is energy that is stored due to an object's position or arrangement. As you climb higher against the force of gravity, your body gains potential energy due to its position—its higher location. The potential energy is converted back to kinetic energy as you move down the slide.
