24. SLOGAN END RACISM ,BUILD PEACE ​


SLOGAN "END RACISM , BUILD PEACE" is a slogan by the International Day of Peace or the International Day of Peace established by the United Nations Resolution on September 21, 1981. This is very much felt necessary to increase the sense of peace in all countries of the world by not discriminating against race.

FURTHER EXPLANATION:

Racism is a belief system or doctrine that holds that the inherent biological differences of the human race determine cultural or individual achievement. It can be said that people who practice racism that a certain race is superior and have the right to rule over other races. Some people who write articles on racism refer to preferences for certain ethnic groups (ethnocentrism), fear of strangers (xenophobia), rejection of racial relationships (miscegenation), and generalizations towards certain groups of people (stereotypes).

In 2022, the United Nations takes the theme of the International Day of Peace as "End racism. Build peace" or means "End Racism, Build Peace". The main goal is to invite the entire world community so that there will be no more acts of racism against fellow human beings. This is what we should further enhance the ties of brotherhood so that peace throughout the world. The slogan of avoiding racism and promoting peace is the main goal for the world community. Because humans who live in this world should not deserve ridicule, moreover the ridicule comes from differences in race or skin color. Happy International Day of Peace, may Peace be upheld and avoid racism between countries. May the world find peace and happiness from God.
