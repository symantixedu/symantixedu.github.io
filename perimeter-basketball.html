19. Lian walked around a basketball court. The court is 28m long and 15m wide. What is the perimeter of the court?​


The perimeter of a rectangle (such as a basketball court) is the sum of the lengths of all its sides.

In this case, the length of the basketball court is 28m and the width is 15m. To find the perimeter, we simply add up the lengths of all four sides:

Perimeter = 2(length + width)

Perimeter = 2(28m + 15m)

Perimeter = 2(43m)

Perimeter = 86m

Therefore, the perimeter of the basketball court is 86 meters.
