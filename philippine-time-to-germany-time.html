9. The Mannerism Period happened during which time period?

A. 1520 - 1580

B. 1401 - 1495

C. 1490's

D. 1520 - 1580

Answer: Mannerism Period happened around early 1520's until the beginning of the 1590's (some says in 1580). It happened between the periods of High Renaissance and the Baroque style. It was during this time that the popular art style was more on using irrational subjects and stuffs like artificial colors and poses, unclear subjects, and strives for more artistry and elegance. So, the answer is either A or D.

10. Which countries were involved in the Early Renaissance?

A. England, Poland, Great Britain

B. Spain, Italy, Rome, America

C. France, Germany, England, Spain

D. England, France, Germany, Norway

Answer: Italy was the most impacted by the popularization of the birth of Renaissance period of art styles, which later on expanded to France and other parts of Europe - such as Germany and England. So, from the selection above, the nearest answer is C. France, Germany, England, Spain. Yes, Spain is included because of the country's connection to Europe in terms of politics. Renaissance and humanistic style was introduced in Spain in the early 15th century (Renaissance in Italy started around early 14th century).

11. The High Renaissance happened during which time period?

A. 1520-1580

Answer: It happened around 1420 to 1527 (some says 1495 - 1520), flourishing for about more or less 35 years. It is centered in Rome, Italy and the three highly artistic figures: Leonardo da Vinci, Michaelangelo, and Raphael. So, from the selection above, the nearest I can guess is B. 1490's.
