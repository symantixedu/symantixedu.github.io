9. How many ways you can select 4 pictures out of 10 pictures of your favorite Korean Pop Star?


COMBINATION

[tex] \red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex] \underline {\mathbb{PROBLEM : }}[/tex]

[tex] \red{••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••}[/tex]

[tex] \underline {\mathbb{ANSWER : }}[/tex]

[tex] \underline {\mathbb{SOLUTION : }}[/tex]

We know that a permutation is define a word " arranging " while combination is " selecting ".

Using the formula for finding the combination

[tex] \rm n = 10 \: \: || \: r = 4[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \rm \: _nC_r = \frac{n!}{r!(n - r)!} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \rm \: _{10}C_4 = \frac{10!}{4!(10 - 4)!} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \rm \: _{10}C_4 = \frac{10 \times 9 \times 7 \times \cancel6!}{4! \times \cancel6!} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \rm \: _{10}C_4= \frac{5040}{4!} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \rm \: _{10}C_4 = \frac{5040}{24} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex] \begin{gathered} \rm \: _{10}C_4= 210 \end{gathered}[/tex]

Therefore, there are 210 ways I can select 4 pictures out of 10 pictures of my favorite Korean Pop Star.
