Is a plain on top of a mountain or a hill. It is also called tableland.

• CHOICES •

A. PLAINB. VOLCANOC. PLATEAUD. MOUNTAINE. VALLEY

• ANSWER •

C. PLATEAU

Plateaus or Plateaux, also called a high plain or a tableland, is an area of a highland consisting of flat terrain that is raised sharply above the surrounding area on at least one side. Often one or more sides have deep hills.
