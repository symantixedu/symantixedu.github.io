16. often recommend as a great way to relax back muscles​


Answer:What is the best way to relax back muscles?

Bend the right knee up and cross it over the left side of your body. Hold in a position that allows you to feel a gentle stretch through the back and buttocks muscles for 20 seconds. Tighten your core muscles and rotate back to center. Repeat three times on each side.

What is the one stretch that relieves back pain?

Knee to Chest Stretch

Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Place both of your hands behind one knee, then slowly pull your knee towards your chest until you can feel the stretch along your lower back and through your hip. Hold, then return your leg to its starting position

How long does back tightness last?

"Most people's back pain dissipates in about a week or two, but if your pain is long-term or chronic, it's time to see a spine specialist."

What causes mid back tightness?

Upper and middle back pain may be caused by: Overuse, muscle strain, or injury to the muscles, ligaments, and discs that support your spine. Poor posture. Pressure on the spinal nerves from certain problems, such as a herniated disc.

How should I sleep with back pain?

For some people, sleeping on their back may be the best position to relieve back pain:

1. Lay flat on your back.

2. Place a pillow underneath your knees and keep your spine neutral. ...

3.You may also place a small, rolled up towel under the small of your back for added support.
