2. In a shop, the cost of 4 pairs of pants, 4 polo shirts, and 2 neckties is 560Php. The cost of 9 pairs of pants, 9 polo shirts, and 6 neckties is 1,290Php. What is the total cost of 1 pair of pants, 1 polo shirt, and 1 necktie?​


Answer:

Let P be the cost of 1 pair of pants, S be the cost of 1 polo shirt, and T be the cost of 1 necktie.

The given information can be written as the following system of equations:

4P + 4S + 2T = 560

9P + 9S + 6T = 1290

We can solve this system of equations using the elimination method. Adding the two equations together, we get:

13P + 13S + 8T = 1860

Subtracting the first equation from the second equation, we get:

5P + 5S + 4T = 730

Dividing both sides of the second equation by 5, we get:

P + S + T = 146

Therefore, the total cost of 1 pair of pants, 1 polo shirt, and 1 necktie is 146 Php.


3. Joshua spent 1200php on a pair of pants and a polo shirt. The cost of the pair of the pants is 600 more than the cost of the polo shirt What is this in mathematic sentence


Answer:

Mathematical sentence:

x + x+600 = 1,200

Step-by-step explanation:

In terms of two variables x and y:

Polo shirt: x

Pair of pants: y

Equation:

x + y = 1,200

In terms of one variable, x:

Polo Shirt: x

Pants: x + 600  ⇒ (a pair of pants is 600 more than the polo shirt, x)

Equation :

x + x+600 = 1,200

To solve for the unit cost:

x + x+600 = 1,200

2x = 1,200 - 600

2x/2 = 600/2

x = 300

Unit cost:

Polo shirt: x = Php300

Pair of pants: x+600  ⇒  300 + 600 = Php900

Check:

300 + 900 = 1,200

1,200 = 1,200
