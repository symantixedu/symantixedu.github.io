1. what is positive and negative lens​


Answer:

A positive focal length means that the focal point of the lens is on the other side of the lens from where the object is placed. On the other hand, a negative focal length means that the focal point is on the same side of the lens as where the object is placed.


2. Is Focal length of concave lens positive?


Answer:

That means that the focal point is on the same side of the lens as the light rays were coming from. ... The distance from the lens to the focal point is called the focal length. For converging lenses, the focal length is always positive, while diverging lenses always have negative focal lengths.

8. at what position from the lens does the object formed real image​


Answer:

like a balls and the object

A real image is formed when the light rays coming from an object converge to a single point, and this point can be located either on a screen or in space. The position of a real image from the lens is determined by the focal length of the lens and the distance of the object from the lens. If the object is located at a distance greater than the focal length, the real image will be located on the same side of the lens as the object, and if the object is located at a distance less than the focal length, the real image will be located on the opposite side of the lens.
