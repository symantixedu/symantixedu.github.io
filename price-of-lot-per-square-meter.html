PRICE PER SQUARE METER==============================Question:

Mr. JB has a rectangular lot that measures 550 meters by 0.75 km. What is the area of the land in square meter. He is selling the whole lot for 206,250,000 pesos,how much is the price per square meter?

Answer:

[tex] \large\boxed{ \sf \orange{P500.00 \: per \: {m}^{2} }}[/tex]

==============================

Solve:

Identify the length and the width of rectangular plot. The given are:

550 meters0.75 kilometers

Since the two unit of measurements are not the same, convert kilometers to meters.

[tex] \begin{aligned} \sf 1 \: km \: & = \sf1000 \: m \\ \sf 1 \times 0.75 \: km & = \sf \: 1000 \times 0.75 \: m \\ \sf0.75 \: km & = \sf750 \: m\end{aligned}[/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

Use the formula of finding the area of the rectangle where the length is multiplied to its width.

[tex] \implies \sf A = l \times w[/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf A = 750m \times 550m[/tex]

[tex]\implies \sf \therefore \boxed{ \sf A = 412 \, 500 {m}^{2} }[/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

That means 412,500m² will cost P206,250,000. (Ang mahal naman). To find how much cost in every square meter (m²), divide the total cost to the Area of the lot.

[tex] \implies \sf \frac{206 , 250 ,000}{412 , 500} = \large \orange{500}\\ [/tex]

==============================

16. 2. Mr. JB has a rectangular lot that measures 550 meters by 0.75 km. What is the area of the land in square meter. He is selling the whole lot for 206, 250,000 pesos, how much is the price per square meter?​


Answer:

The area of the land in square meters is 412, 500.

500 pesos is the price per square meter of the land.

Step-by-step explanation:

                                        Word Problem

Since the first question asks for the area of the land in "square meter", we need to convert 0.75 kilometers to meters.

Steps in Converting the UnitsChoose the appropriate conversion factors.Multiply the given unit measure by the conversion factor. Simplify the product.

                     [tex]0.75\:\text{km}\times\left(\begin{aligned}\text{Conversion}\\\text{Factor}\end{aligned}\right)[/tex]

Now, we know that 1 km = 1000 m, so our conversion factor should be [tex]\frac{1000\:\text{m}}{1\:\text{km}}[/tex]. We intended to have a unit km in the denominator so that during the multiplication process, we can cancel the km unit.

                    [tex]\begin{aligned}0.75\:\text{km}\times\left(\frac{1000\:\text{m}}{1\:\text{km}}\right)&=0.75\times 1000\:\text{m}\\&=750\:\text{m}\end{aligned}[/tex]

The area of a rectangle is given by the formula, [tex]A = l \times w[/tex]. So, to solve for the area, substitute the value of [tex]l=750\:\text{m}[/tex] and [tex]w=550\:\text{m}[/tex] into the area formula then simplify.

                 [tex]\begin{aligned}A&=l\times w\\&=750\:\text{m}\times550\:\text{m}\\&=412, 500\:\text{m}^2\end{aligned}[/tex]

So, the area of the rectangular lot is 412, 500 square meters.

If he is selling the whole land for 206, 250, 000 pesos, then to solve the price per square meter is given by the formula, [tex]C=\frac{\text{Price of the whole land}}{\text{Area of the whole land}}[/tex].

So, to solve for the price per square meter, substitute the price of the whole land and the area of the whole land into the equation.

           [tex]\begin{aligned}C&=\frac{206,250,000}{412,500}\\&\boxed{C=500}\end{aligned}[/tex]

Therefore, the price per square meter is 500 pesos.
