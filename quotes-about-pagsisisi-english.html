16. nasa huli ang pagsisisi translate in English noooowwww!!!!


Nasa huli ang pagsisisi can be translated in English as "regret is always at the end". There are choices that we make that do not turn into something good. That is when regrets consume us. Our regrets tend to be one of the biggest life lessons. Life lets you have your choices. You either choose to be good, or bad. But every decision you have made comes the consequences at the end.  You have to experience it first and learned your lessons at the end. Embrace the lessons you learned. There’s nothing wrong with having regrets, it’s just a matter of learning.

Things to do  that you will never regret at the end

Studies have shown that there are several things dying people regret that they did not do when they have the opportunity and the time. Here are some of the stuff you can do in life so that you will never regret at the end:

Pursue your dreams and accomplish more goals Spend more time with people you care about Express yourself more Learn and explore more Take care of your finances Choose to be happy Live to the fullest and never waste time

Things to avoid regrets later on in your life

Living full of regret is not healthy at all, here are some ways to avoid regrets later in your life.

Don’t follow someone else’s dreams Stop taking your loved ones for granted Stop pretending to be someone else Don’t burn all your bridges Always tell the truth Live in the moment Don’t give up on true love
