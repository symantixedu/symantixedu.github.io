9. what is pearl harbor?


Pearl Harbor is a U.S. naval base near Honolulu, Hawaii, that was the scene of a devastating surprise attack by Japanese forces on December 7, 1941. Just before 8 a.m. on that Sunday morning, hundreds of Japanese fighter planes descended on the base, where they managed to destroy or damage nearly 20 American naval vessels, including eight battleships, and over 300 airplanes. More than 2,400 Americans died in the attack, also civilians, and another  1,000 people were wounded. The next day, after the assault, President Franklin D. Roosevelt asked Congress to declare war on Japan.
