Meaning of 5Rs

Explanation:

1.Reduce

2.Reuse

3.Recycle

4.Repair

5.Recover

Answer:

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, Recover,repair

The City encourages the four principles of the Eastern Metropolitan Regional Council’s R-gang reduce, reuse, recycle,repair and recover.

REDUCE

is to limit the amount of waste you create in the first place. This includes buying products with less packaging.

REUSE

means to use something again that you would normally throw away (eg. Glass jar for food or plastic bags for bin liners.)

RECYCLE

means the product goes through a mechanical process to change its form. This is only recommended when reducing and reusing are not possible.

RECOVER

is to convert waste into resources (such as electricity, heat, compost and fuel) through thermal and biological means. Resource Recovery occurs after reduce, reuse and recycle have been attempte

REPAIR

fix or mend (a thing suffering from damage or a fault).
