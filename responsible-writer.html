18. How can I be a responsible school paper writer? 1._____________2._____________​


Answer:

Here are some ways to be a responsible school paper writer:

1. **Research thoroughly and accurately**: Make sure to gather information from reliable sources and verify the accuracy of the information before including it in your paper.

2. **Cite your sources**: Always give credit where credit is due by properly citing your sources using the appropriate citation format.

3. **Avoid plagiarism**: Plagiarism is a serious offense in academic writing. Make sure to always use your own words and ideas, and if you do use someone else's work, make sure to properly cite it.

4. **Proofread and edit**: Before submitting your paper, make sure to carefully proofread and edit it for errors in grammar, spelling, punctuation, and formatting.

5. **Follow guidelines**: Make sure to follow any guidelines or instructions provided by your teacher or institution for formatting and submitting your paper.
