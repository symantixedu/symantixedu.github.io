This is a buffable wax used for vinyl, linoleum, and rubber.

Emulsion Wax is a buffable wax that may be used on vinyl, linoleum, and rubber tile floors. It may also be used on marble and concrete floors. A wax emulsion is a water-based stable mixture of one or more waxes. Waxes and water are typically incompatible, but with the application of surfactants and a creative preparation method, they can be brought together stably. Because wax is solid at average temperature, a wax emulsion should technically be called wax dispersion. The actual process is called emulsification because it takes place above the melting point of the wax, hence the name wax emulsion. Wax dispersion is commonly utilized in solvent-based systems. A diverse range of emulsions based on various waxes and mixes are available.

27. Ipaliwanag ang pagkakaiba ng linoleum printing,rubber printing at wood cut printing.​


Answer:

Linoluem Printiing : isang paraan ng pag-print ng lunas, na nagsasangkot ng pag-ukit ng pagguhit sa isang malambot na bloke ng linoleum, pinagsama ang isang manipis na layer ng tinta sa ibabaw nito, at pinindot ang papel sa itaas upang ilipat ang imahe. Ang mga lugar kung saan ka nakaukit ay madalas na lumilikha ng magagandang mga resulta.

Rubber Printing : isang opaque base na tumataas sa pagiging naka-set upang magbigay ng isang nakataas na epekto sa pag-print.

Wood cut Printing ; ay isang pamamaraan para sa pagpi-print ng teksto, mga imahe o pattern sa mga tela at papel. .
