21. Identify the use of future tense in the sentence below. I'm meeting my mother at the airport tomorrow. Select one: a. Predictions/statements of fact b. Scheduled event c. Intentions d. Arrangement


The use of future tense

I'm meeting my mother at the airport tomorrow.

The answer is "D. Arrangement"

An arrangement is a plan for the future that you have already thought about it or decided and discussed with someone else.

This often use the present continuous tense "subject + 'be' = verb-ing" to talk about future arrangements. They are very easy to spot, since future arrangements have gerund. Gerund is a verb with -ing tacked to its end.

Example:I'm meeting my mother at the airport tomorrow.Our relatives are visiting us this Christmas.Sorry, I can't stay after school today; I'm playing chess with Britney.My sister's going to the dentist tomorrow.Are you doing anything on Monday morning?
