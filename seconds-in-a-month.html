Problem:

marvie saved 1755.45 from her earnings from selling calamansi juice each month.On the first month,she saved 585.45 on the second month,she saved 195.45 less than the first month.And on the third month she saved twice as much as the second month.How much money did she save in the second month?and on the third month?

Asked:

The amount of money was saved on the second and third month.

Given Facts:

There was ₱1,755.45 saved from the earnings from selling clamansi juice each month. ₱585.45 was saved on the first month. 195.45 less than the first month was saved on the second month. Twice as much as the second month was saved on the third month.

Operation/s:

Multiplication, Subtraction

Number Sentence:

(585.45-195.45)2=x

Solution:

[tex](585.45 - 195.45)2 = x \\ x = 390 \times 2 \\ x = 780[/tex]

Answers:

Second Month: ₱390.00

Third Month: ₱780.00
