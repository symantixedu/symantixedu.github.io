7. 1. What is the secret sentence on math​


Answer:

I have in my mind to collect a “You might be a mathematician if…” statements, a la Jeff Foxworthy. I only got as far as the following:

“If you’re baking a cake, and the recipe calls for four whole eggs, you hesitate before adding them.”

The secret to learning math is simple: in math, what’s written is what is meant. You’d think this would make math easy. The problem is that most of us have been trained to look for hidden meanings in everything, and to assume that those hidden meanings are what is meant. But to a mathematician, what’s there is what is meant. So a whole egg, to a mathematician, is the whole egg, and if the recipe calls for four whole eggs, you’d use four whole eggs, shells and all.

Let’s take another example. Some months ago, there was a notorious meme that blasted a common core teacher for marking a student down for the following: “Find  5×3 . The student wrote  5×3=5+5+5 .

This is incorrect, and the teacher was right for marking the student down. The reason is that  5×3  has a very specific meaning based on the definition of multiplication: it is the sum of five 3s. So  5×3=3+3+3+3+3 . What the student wrote, the sum of three 5s, is actually  3×5.  

Aren’t they the “same thing”? Not to a mathematician. For that matter, a lawyer would also reject them as being the same, for the same reason:  5×3  has a clearly defined meaning, and anything different is not the same thing. (You may interpret them as being the same…and that’s where law and mathematics diverge…but the starting point is that any difference is significant)

You might say that this is being nitpicky. Exactly right…because that’s what you need to do to be successful at mathematics. Consider this: If you think  5×3  is three 5s, then you will think  5×2  is two 5s,  5×11  is eleven 5s, and then… 5x  is  x  is 5s. But while you can picture three 5s, or two 5s, or eleven 5s, can you picture  x  5s? You don’t know what  x  is, so you have some undetermined number of 5s floating around.

But if you follow the definition and see that  5×3  is five 3s, then  5×2  is five 2s,  5×11  is five 11s, and  5x  is five  x s:  x+x+x+x+x . Then  5x+3x  is five  x s together with three  x s, which means eight  x s all together, or  8x .

15. Topic: The Secret of SuccessWrite your sentence outline here.Thesis Statement:​


The Secret of Success

Sentence Outline:

1. Have a Purpose

2. Establish Good Interaction

3. Appreciate Every Work Done

4. Firm in Action

5. Enjoy Failure

6. Always Be Disciplined

Thesis Statement:​

1. Have a Purpose

Everyone who lives each has their own goals. Another thing that must also be instilled is the courage to step across the road to success. By having clear life goals, this will help you to always focus on achieving success. Even a goal can be a strength in itself when you are at the point of fatigue. So try to remember the goals of success that are currently being lived.

2. Establish Good Interaction

What is the use of intelligence, without proper social skills. Of course this will be difficult to achieve success. In fact, relationships play a very important role in success. By having many friends, you will get many benefits in relation to achieving your goals. Whether it's in the form of support to knowledge, even friends are one of the keys to success. Therefore, establish as much interaction as possible. Never allow yourself to be confined to solitude. Because, it will prevent you from achieving success.

3. Appreciate Every Work Done

Whether it's a task that feels light though, it should still be done wholeheartedly so that the results are maximized. Getting into the habit of always appreciating every task given is a powerful way to build integrity within yourself. If it is so, then your steps to achieve success will be easy to pass. Assume that every task you do is an asset to you in the future.

4. Firm in Action

Being decisive when acting is the secret to success. This is realized by world famous figures. From now on, make assertiveness a part of yourself. Both in small things to big things, don't be wishy-washy in making decisions. Being assertive also requires a process. Before you can be firm with others, be firm with yourself first. In being firm when making decisions, receiving input from a number of parties also cannot be ignored. You still need input from other people, including coworkers. Because, for some successful people, feedback is the initial key to fixing everything.

5. Enjoy Failure

This is something that some people have not yet experienced. For successful people, failure is something that has been anticipated in advance, and is also believed to be part of the process to success. Therefore, when you are experiencing failure, do the same as world leaders do. That is trying to enjoy the failure and make it as a whip to be more active in the pursuit of success.

6. Always Be Disciplined

If discipline is not a part of you, then say goodbye to success. People who tend to ignore discipline will find it difficult to achieve success. Precisely discipline is the secret of one's success. From now on try to instill a sense of discipline from within. Do everything according to schedule.

22. Determine whether the following sentences are dangling or not. Wishing doom on her co-worker, secret thoughts travelled through Sophia’s mind.


Answer:

it is not a dangling sentence

Dangling Sentences

Answer:

The given sentence is not a dangling one. The phrase wishing doom on her co-worker was correctly placed, hence, correctly modifies the secret thoughts that travelled through Sophia's mind. At first, it is confusing but if we are to break the sentences down to several pieces, we will realize that it is not a dangling one.  

Dangling Sentences

A sentence is considered to be a dangling one if the sentence isn't clear on what the given modifiers modifies in the sentence.

Steps of identifying a danging sentence

To identify if the sentence contains a dangling modifier, try the following steps:

Read the and analyze the sentenceSee if you are able to understand it upon the first readingIf the sentence confuses you, write down the parts of the sentenceCheck if the parts of speech such as noun, pronoun, adjective, adverbs, etc are correctly placed in the sentence

Correcting Dangling Sentences

Below are the ways to correct a dangling sentence

Once you are sure that the sentences are dangling, pick the phrase that alters the meaning of the sentenceModify the sentence by rewriting it again and placing the dangling modifier in the correct positionRewrite the sentence and read it aloud. Have someone check it for you.
