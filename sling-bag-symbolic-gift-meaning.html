15. carlo had 1450. He spent 2/5 of it on the cd game. he then spent 1/3 of the remaining money on a sling bag. how much was left with carlo?


Solution:
Money spent on CD game: 2/3 of Total Money
(2/5) (1450) = 580  peosos, amount spent on CD game

Subtract 580 from 1450 to get the amount of remaining money:
1450 - 580 = 870  pesos, remaining money

Amount of money spent on sling bag: 1/3 of remaining money:
(1/3) (870) = 290 pesos,  money spent on sling bag

Add the money spent, then subtract it from Total Money to get what was left with Carlo:

1450 - (580 + 290) = money left
1450 - 870 = 580 pesos, money left with Carlo

Final Answer: Carlo was left with 580 pesos.

Answer:

Step 1. Cut out the fabric pieces for the sling bag,To make this sling bag cut out 2 bag body fabric pieces and one flap piece from the outer fabric and 2 bag body fabric pieces and one flap piece from the lining fabric. You also need a 38 inch long bag strap – you can use webbing for this as I have. You also need a magnetic snap (or velcro) for closure.

The dart can be drawn 2.5 inches from the sides.

Cut from the outer fabric.And the lining fabric.

Step 2. Prepare the main body of the sling bag

Sew the dart closed for the outer bag pieces and lining pieces.Assemble the bag. Join the 2 pieces of the main body . Do the same for the lining.

Step 3. Prepare the flap for the sling bag.

Separate the magnetic snap.Take the projecting part and attach it to the lining flap. Please interface the back where you will be attaching the magnetic snap – other than this use another piece of interfacing or fabric to give some more strength to the area.

Keep the two pieces of the flap – outer fabric and lining rightsides to the inside and sew along the outer edge as in the picture below. Clip the seam allowance.

Step 4.Turn the flap right side out.

Insert the flap on the back of the bag body between the lining and outer fabric . Refer picture below – lining part of the flap and the bag wil come together (Please make sure that this is done on the back part of the bag). Pin in place.

Insert the bag strap the same way – between the outer bag and the lining.Pin the handle edge on the sides of the bag.

Ensure everything is tucker inside well. Now sew the the top edge of the outer bag body and the lining bag together. Leave 3 inches unstitched at the front of the bag (without the flap) . Give two stitching over the handle and flap.

Turn the bag rightside out through the hole you have left unstitched.

Step 6. Attach the other part of the magnetic snap

You have to find the exact position to attach the other part of the snap. Mark this place. Ensure that it will close properly. Insert hand through the unstitched hole on the top edge and make holes on the outer bag fabric and attach the snap.A small and cute bag.
