SMART objectives are Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-related. S for Specific to not waste your valuable resources on things not relevant to your research. M for Measurable to have a realistic requirement that you can show what you have achieved. 13. Anti-intellectualism: how smart-shaming affects the self-esteem of the student?​


Answer:

Anti-intellectualism, the manifestation of disrespect and lack of interest of individuals for intellectual pursuits and critical thinking, has become a constant thread not only in the corporate world but also among students. Its long-term eﬀect will be very excruciating, especially in the Philippine economy. This quantitative correlational research investigated the extent of relationship between the anti-intellectual attitudes and level of self-esteem or the individual’s overall sense of self-worth or personal value of one hundred and ninety-four (194) senior high school students from Fiat Lux Academe-Cavite, Philippines. The researchers made use of the Student’s Anti-Intellectualism Scale and State Self-Esteem Scale to measure the two variables. Analyzed data revealed an average level of attitude of anti-intellectualism and a moderate level of self-esteem among the participants. Furthermore, there is a significant, inverse, and moderate correlation between the two variables (Pearson= -0.50; Spearman Correlation = -0.44). Thus, students with high level of self-esteem have low anti-intellectual attitudes. Conversely, students exhibiting low level of anti-intellectual attitudes tend to have higher self esteem. The researchers also utilized other statistical techniques such as the t Statistic and linear regression.

22. Can someone please make me a Narrative Text about Smart Shaming in the Philippines?​


Smart shaming is a prevalent issue in the Philippines. One day, a student who always excelled in class was mocked by his classmates for being too smart. They called him "nerd" and "bookworm" as if it was a bad thing. The student felt embarrassed and ashamed, and he started to doubt his abilities. He stopped participating in class discussions and tried to hide his intelligence. This behavior continued until one day, a teacher noticed the student's sudden change in behavior and talked to him. The teacher encouraged the student to be proud of his intelligence and to never let anyone make him feel ashamed of it. The student gained confidence and started to participate in class again. The teacher also talked to the other students about the harmful effects of smart shaming and how it can discourage students from reaching their full potential. From then on, the class became more supportive of each other's achievements, and smart shaming was no longer tolerated.

24. E, di Ikaw na ang Matalino! Isang Pagsusuri sa Penomenon ng Smart-Shaming pananaw pls​


Ang "smart-shaming" ay isang phenomenon na kung saan ang isang tao ay ikinahihiya o inaapi dahil sa kanilang talino, kaalaman, o pagkaintindi. Ito ay maaaring mangyari sa mga paaralan, trabaho, at iba pang lugar na may mga taong nagkukumpetensya o nakikipagtalo tungkol sa kanilang kakayahan. Ang mga taong nakakaranas ng smart-shaming ay maaaring madama ang pagkabigo, kawalan ng kumpiyansa sa sarili, at pagkawala ng interes sa pag-aaral.

Ang pagiging matalino ay dapat na itinuturing na isang kagandahang katangian, ngunit ang smart-shaming ay nagdudulot ng negatibong epekto sa mga taong nagtitiyaga na maging mas magaling. Sa halip na pahalagahan at magbigay ng inspirasyon sa mga taong nakapagtapos ng pag-aaral, ang smart-shaming ay nagpapakita ng kawalan ng respeto at pagpapahalaga sa kanilang mga kakayahan.

Ang mga taong nagiging biktima ng smart-shaming ay maaaring magdulot ng mental health problems tulad ng depression at anxiety. Kaya't mahalaga na bigyan ng suporta at pagpapahalaga ang mga taong may magandang kaisipan at talino upang masiguro na hindi sila magdusa sa mga pang-aapi o pagsupil ng kanilang mga kakayahan.

Upang maiwasan ang smart-shaming, mahalaga na magkaroon ng kultura ng pagpapahalaga sa kaalaman at pag-unlad. Dapat na ituring na kahanga-hanga ang pagkakaroon ng talino at hindi dapat itong maging dahilan upang magdulot ng anumang uri ng diskriminasyon o pang-aapi. Sa halip, dapat itong maging dahilan upang magbigay ng inspirasyon at suporta sa mga taong nagpupursigi na magkaroon ng kaalaman at magtagumpay sa kanilang mga layunin sa buhay.
