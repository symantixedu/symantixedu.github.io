GivenPhp. 10, 000 spent moneyPhp. 450 worth of souvenir75 items of souvenir was sold

ProblemTotal money make by the government (profit)

Solution

Total Gain = Number of items × Price of each item

Profit = Total Gain - Total Cost

Profit = 450(75) - 10, 000

Profit = 33, 750 - 10, 000

Profit = 23, 750

Therefore, the money make (total profit earned) is Php. 23, 750.

27. What are your recommendations about souvenir products that could help us to improve it.​


When it comes to choosing the right souvenir products, there are a few things that you should keep in mind. First, you want to make sure that the products you select are representative of the place you are visiting. For example, if you are visiting a beach town, you may want to purchase souvenirs that are beach-themed, such as seashells or sand dollars. If you are visiting a historic city, you may want to purchase souvenirs that are representative of the city's history, such as a keychain with the city's name or a mug with a picture of the city's skyline.

Second, you want to make sure that the souvenirs you select are of good quality. This is especially important if you plan on giving the souvenirs to friends or family members. You want to make sure that the souvenirs will last and that they will be something that the recipient will actually want to use or display.

Third, you want to make sure that the souvenirs you select are reasonably priced. This is important both for your own budget and for the budget of the person you are giving the souvenir to. You don't want to spend too much money on a souvenir that no one will want to keep, but you also don't want to purchase a cheap souvenir that will fall apart easily.

Here are a few recommendations for souvenir products that meet all of the criteria above:

A keychain with the name or logo of the place you are visitingA mug with a picture of the skyline or other iconic image of the place you are visitingA t-shirt with the name or logo of the place you are visitingA postcard with a picture of the place you are visitingA magnet with the name or logo of the place you are visitingA snow globe with a picture of the place you are visitingA shot glass with the name or logo of the place you are visiting
