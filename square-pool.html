1. a square meter pool is to be tiled by square tiles. How many square centimeters of tiles will be needed to completely tile the pool ?​


Step-by-step explanation:

To calculate the area of the pool, we need to know the length and width of the pool. If we assume that the pool is a perfect square, then the length and width are equal. Let's call the length/width of the pool "x".

The area of the pool is then x * x, which simplifies to x^2.

If we want to tile the pool with square tiles, each tile will have an area of s^2, where "s" is the side length of the tile.

We don't know the size of the tiles, but let's say they are "y" centimeters on each side.

To cover the entire pool with tiles, we need to divide the total area of the pool by the area of each tile:

x^2 / y^2

We can simplify this by converting the units to centimeters (since we are given the pool size in square meters). Since 1 meter is equal to 100 centimeters, we know that:

1 square meter = 100 cm x 100 cm = 10,000 square cm

So the area of the pool in square centimeters is:

x^2 * 10,000

And the area of each tile in square centimeters is:

y^2

So the number of tiles needed to completely tile the pool is:

(x^2 * 10,000) / y^2

This gives us the answer in square centimeters of tiles needed to completely tile the pool.

Since 1 meter is equal to 100 centimeters, then 1 square meter is equal to 100 cm x 100 cm = 10,000 square centimeters.

So the 20 square meter pool is equal to 20 x 10,000 = 200,000 square centimeters.

Since we are tiling the pool with square tiles, we need to find out how many square centimeters are in each tile. Let's say the length of one side of each square tile is x centimeters.

The area of one tile is then x cm x x cm = x^2 square centimeters.

To find how many tiles are needed to cover the pool, we need to divide the total area of the pool by the area of one tile:

Number of tiles = total area of pool / area of one tile

Number of tiles = 200,000 square centimeters / x^2 square centimeters

Therefore, the amount of tiles needed to completely tile the pool is 200,000/x^2.
