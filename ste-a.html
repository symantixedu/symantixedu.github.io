30. why do you want to join STE class?​


The reason for joining the STE class is because of the urgency at this time, namely regarding the needs of the times.

This is so that students' knowledge and understanding of science, technology, and engineering can grow. This information can then be utilized to solve problems and make decisions for improvement. This is how Science, Technology, and Engineering (STE)-based learning is intended to be employed. character traits. Additionally, we live in the age of technology. We must, therefore, be able to adapt if we don't want to fall behind.

This STE class is crucial to incorporate into the teaching and learning processes of students because it offers a number of benefits, such as helping to develop the next generation's capacity for adapting to change, fostering the growth of innovations that affect human life, and fostering student interest in the fields of Science, Technology, and Technology. Boosting student understanding of STE, connecting learning to real-world situations, assisting students in actively developing their own self-concepts, and more.

The needs of the present, particularly the necessity for pupils to develop 21st century thinking abilities like critical thinking, creativity, cooperation, and communication, can also be tied to STE classes. In keeping with this, learning in STE classes also calls for students to be able to recognize problems, come up with solutions, work with classmates to find solutions, communicate clearly, and respond to one another's ideas.

Students are encouraged to develop systematic and iterative approaches to designing things, processes, and systems to satisfy human needs and wants in order to put their STE learning into practice (engineering). Engineering components in STE may begin with a need, desire, or problem and quantifiable criteria that are then put to the test to find restrictions or limitations.

By using a set of procedures that engineers use to design a specific product or technology to meet the stated requirements, these innovation development activities can be carried out in the classroom.
