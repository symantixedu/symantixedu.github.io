27. Netiquette is considered _____ if it is strictly upheld in an online community


The answer is Formal netiquette.

Netiquette means proper or appropriate online behavior when you use the internet. But one netiquette is considered formal when you do it in an online community such as online message boards (or forums), groups (like group chats or online groups) and other form of online gatherings or community. Formal netiquette are sometimes following rules and guidelines set by online administrators or website owners.

Here are examples of formal netiquette: Respect all members, admins and guests of the group. Avoid posting topic irrelevant to the thread, group or chat. Do not post consecutively or you will be marked as a spammer. Avoid bullying others or posting offensive messages.
