Kadalasan ito nangyayari pagkatapos ng 5 hanggang 10 araw pagkatapos ng pagtatanim, ngunit maaaring mas matagal sa malamig na panahon. 300,000000

Step-by-step explanation:

= 15,000

15,000x500

=75,00000

= 300,000000

300,000000 sunflowers will be reproduced at the end of four generations.

30. The diameter of a sunflower clock in the park is 6 m. what is the area of the sunflower clock​


Area of Circle is given by the formula:

A = πr²

where

A = Area

π = 3.14

r = radius = 6/2 = 3 m*

* if length is given in diameter, simply divide diameter by 2 to get the radius

solve for A,

A = πr²

A = 3.14 (3)²

A = 3.14 (9)

A =28.26 m²

So the area of the sunflower clock is 28.26 m²
