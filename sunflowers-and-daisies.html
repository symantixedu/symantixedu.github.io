3. A gardener planted sunflowers, roses and daisies in his garden in the ration of 1:2:4. If he had 98 flowers, how many sunflower, roses and daisies were planted? 18. SUNFLOWER= 19. ROSES 20. DAISIESTamng sagot ho dpat​


Answer:

A gardener planted sunflowers, roses and daisies in his garden in the ration of 1:2:4. If he had 98 flowers, how many sunflowers, roses and daisies were planted?

1:2:4 is the ratio of sunflower, roses, and daisies respectively. And if he had 98 flowers, what are the numbers of each flower?

Let x be the number.

1x + 2x + 4x  = 98

7x = 98

Divide both sides by 7.

[tex]\frac{7x}{7} =\frac{98}{7}[/tex]

x = 14

Then,

Sunflower: 1(x) = 1 (14) = 14

Roses: 2(x) = 2(14) = 28

Daisies: 4(x) = 2(14) = 56

Checking:

14 + 28 + 56 = 98

98 = 98

Final Answer:

There are 14 sunflowers, 28 roses, and 56 daisies in his garden.

13. Which of the following is NOT an example of Fibonacci numbers found in nature? *A.spirals on a sunflowerB.the number of petals on a daisyC.pinecone spiralsD.a mountain range.​


Which of the following is NOT an example of Fibonacci numbers found in nature?

A.spirals on a sunflower

B.the number of petals on a daisy

C.pinecone spirals

D.a mountain range

The answer is d. a mountain range.

Fibonacci sequence Each number in the sequence is formed by adding the previous two together and is named after Leonardo of Pisa, a 13th-century Italian mathematician known as Fibonacci. It begins at 1 1 2 3 5 8 13 21 and continues indefinitely.

Explanation: Spirals on the flowers In the spirals formed by individual flowers in various flowers like daisies, sunflowers, cauliflowers, and broccoli, we can easily find the Fibonacci numbers. If you look closely enough at a sunflower, you'll notice that its center isn't merely made up of thousands of seeds arranged in a random pattern. Eventually, a pattern of multiple clockwise and anti-clockwise spirals will emerge.

Number of Petals on Daisy It also forms a Fibonacci sequence but not all flowers form a Fibonacci sequence.

Pinecone Spirals It is also a Fibonacci sequence since it has a pattern. Usually, it has 8 clockwise spirals and 13 counter-clockwise spirals.

Mountain Ranges It is not a Fibonacci sequence because it does not have one pattern that will make a Fibonacci sequence.
