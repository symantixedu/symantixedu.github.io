19. Center at (-2,3) tangent to x-axis with solution


Answer:

(x + 2)² + (y - 3)² = 9

Step-by-step explanation:

Center (h, k): (-2, 3)  

h = -2

k = 3

The circle is tangent to x-axis, therefore:

Radius = |k| = |3| = 3

Equation of the circle when center (h,k) is not at origin (0,0):

(x - h)² + (y - k)² = r²

Substitute:

(x - (-2))² + (y - (3))² = (3)²

(x + 2)² + (y - 3)² = 9
