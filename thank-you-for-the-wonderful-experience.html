FORMULA

[tex]x - 4y = 8[/tex]

SOLVE A SOLUTION TO X

Move the rest of the expression except x term to the right side and replace the sign

[tex]x - 4y - ( - 4y) = 8 - ( - 4y)[/tex]

Organize the expression

[tex]x = 8 - ( - 4y)[/tex]

Organize the expression

[tex]x = 4y + 8[/tex]

Answer is

[tex]x = 4y + 8[/tex]

SOLVE A SOLUTION TO Y

Move the rest of the expression except y term to the right side and replace the sign

[tex] - 4y = 8 - x[/tex]

Organize the expression

[tex] - 4y = - x + 8[/tex]

Change the sign of both side of the expression

[tex]4y = x - 8[/tex]

Divide both side by the same number

[tex]y =(x - 8) \div 4[/tex]

Convert division to multiplication

[tex]y = (x - 8) \times \frac{1}{4} [/tex]

Multiply each term in parentheses by 1/4

[tex]y = \frac{1}{4} x - 8 \times \frac{1}{4} [/tex]

Solve the product of rational number

[tex]y = \frac{1}{4} x - 2[/tex]

The answer is

[tex]y = \frac{1}{4} x - 2[/tex]
