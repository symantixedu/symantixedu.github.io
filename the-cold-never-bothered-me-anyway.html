29. God has forgotten,shows no concern,never bothers to look.What is your insight about the quotation?​


Answer:

I don't think God will ever forget us. We believe he has failed, shows no concern, and never bothers to look. We, humans, have a tendency to lose faith in Him. For example, suppose we were in the midst of a huge problem, and we believe that God is nowhere to be found to help us get out of it. However, it is us who fail to trust in His plans. Maybe we think that way because we are so sad and miserable that nothing is going as planned. We were sometimes consumed by our sad thoughts, which led us to believe that God is powerless to intervene.
