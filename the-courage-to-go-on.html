Being courageous means being able to deal with and face one’s fears without hesitation and reservation.

Based on the statement, we can only know how further we can go if we try our opportunities and risk what we have.  Settling is not a courageous man’s mindset.  He or she aims for greatness, and that is achieved through trying and risking.  Even if the journey is uncertain, a courageous man goes over and beyond his or her limit.  Failure is never his or her fear because having courage is breaking boundaries in order to achieve a higher purpose and understanding of oneself including one’s circumstances.

21. in what aspect of life you are courageous? Do you think this is morally courageous​


Answer:

If you're like most people, you probably equate courage with fearlessness, but that's a faulty interpretation. In fact, courage is taking action in spite of the fear you feel. Courage is the willingness to respond fearlessly despite the anxiety and worry that might be tugging at you.

In fact, one of the best ways to be courageous is to understand what you're afraid of and then refuse to allow that fear to paralyze you. Because if you let it, fear has the power to stop you from moving forward, taking risks, and making the most of opportunities. Meanwhile, being courageous allows you to take chances, pursue your dreams, and get what you want out of life.
