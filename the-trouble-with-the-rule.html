Trolling is related to the usage of internet, wherein a person intentionally makes unruly comments in an online community to trigger the reactions and emotions of other people.

Definition of Troll Troll is a verb that is related to the usage of the internet. If a person trolls, he/she intentionally makes unruly comments in an online community to trigger the reactions and emotions of other people. Troll involves deliberately breaking the rules in an online community to cause trouble or to get attention. Troll is an internet slang. Examples of Trolls

To better explain the concept of troll, here are three examples of trolling:

Leaving an insulting, below-the-belt comments on the internet to bully someone (e.g. calling other people names) Posting off-topic comments and messages in an online group to trigger the emotions of members Posting articles or videos to offend someone or argue with other people
