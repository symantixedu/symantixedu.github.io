8. a bag contains 128 marbles some white and some transparent the ratio of white marbles to transparent ones is 3:5 how many transparent marbles are there


Answer:

80 transparent marbles

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: Calculate the total from the given ratio: 3+5 =8

Step 2: multiply the given total amount of marbles to the ratio of transparent marbles over the total from the given ratio

[tex]128(\frac{5}{8})[/tex] = 80 transparent marbles

therefore, the remaining are white marbles: 128 -80 = 48

to check: get the lowest term of the number of white marbles over transparent marbles and check if it is the same with the given ratio

48/80= 3/5 ↔3:5

THE DIGESTION

Answer with Explanation:

1. Which material in the activity represented the stomach?

The plastic bag which is used in the activity represents the stomach.

2. What happened to the biscuits and the bread when you squeezed the plastic bag?

The biscuits and the bread slowly dissolve as the plastic bag is squeezed.

3. What happened when calamansi juice was added to the mixture and then you squeezed the plastic bag?

When the calamansi juice was added to the mixture while the plastic bag was squeezed, the calamansi juice helped hasten the dissolving process of the biscuits and bread.

4. What is represented by the mixture of water, calamansi juice, and pieces of bread or biscuits?

The mixture of water, calamansi juice and pieces of bread and biscuits represents the mixture of the food and water that we consumed which are being dissolved in the stomach with the aid of gastric and pancreatic juices.

5. Why does the stomach need to soften the food that we eat?

It is necessary for the stomach to soften the food that we eat because it will be easier for the small intestines to absorb the nutrients from the food we eat.

6. What do you call the action done by the stomach to turn the food into soluble materials?

The process that the stomach does in turning food into soluble materials is called digestion.

7. What have you learned from the activity?

From the activity, I learned that the stomach is an involuntary hollow muscular organ that softens the food that we eat. As we eat food and as long as they reach the stomach, they will be slowly broken down into more soluble parts with the help of pancreatic juices. Like the representation of calamansi juice, the pancreatic juices are acidic. The process that the stomach does to the food we eat is called the digestion process.

23. what is example of leather bag transparent translucent opaque​


Answer:

Leather is generally not transparent, translucent, or opaque since it is an opaque material. However, if you are referring to leather bags that have transparent or translucent components or features, here are some examples:

1. Transparent leather bag - Some leather bags may have transparent plastic or vinyl windows that allow you to see the contents of the bag. An example of this is a clear leather tote bag with a transparent PVC panel on one side.

2. Translucent leather bag - There are also leather bags that have a slightly see-through quality, allowing some light to pass through but not revealing the contents completely. An example of this is a leather handbag with laser-cut perforations that create a semi-translucent effect.

3. Opaque leather bag - Most leather bags are opaque, meaning that they do not allow any light to pass through. An example of this is a classic leather messenger bag or a leather backpack that has a solid, non-transparent construction.
