Answer:

It actually depends.

Explanation:

There are some products that doesn't expire after the best before date. Technically, the best before dates are the dates set by the manufacturer when testing their products, if those products are not in the best quality after that specific date, they would consider it as best before date. For example, if you brought a cake and saw that the best before date indicates to eat it before Tuesday, that means that after Tuesday you can still eat it(unless it has molds) but you can't expect it to be in the best condition. The reason for this is, companies are making sure that the things you buy from them is in the best condition, therefore you can't say anything bad about them. Another reason is that they want people to dispose the products in fear of food poisoning so that people would buy more. So it depends, some companies do these things and some genuinely knew that their products contains ingredients that may expire sooner than any other ingredients.
