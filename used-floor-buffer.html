To use a vacuum cleaner, first ensure that the vacuum bag or canister is empty and properly secured. Plug the vacuum in and turn it on, and adjust the height of the vacuum head to the appropriate level for the type of flooring being cleaned. Slowly move the vacuum in a back-and-forth motion, overlapping each stroke slightly to ensure all debris is picked up. Pay special attention to corners and baseboards, and switch attachments as needed for upholstery or other surfaces. To use a floor buffer, first ensure that the pad is properly installed and that the buffer is adjusted to the correct height for the flooring type. Plug in the buffer and turn it on, and slowly move it across the floor in a circular motion, overlapping each stroke slightly to ensure a consistent finish. Use a gentle touch and avoid pressing too hard, and switch to a cleaner or polish as needed for a deeper clean or shine. Remember to always wear appropriate safety gear, such as gloves and eye protection, when using cleaning equipment.
