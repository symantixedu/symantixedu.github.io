21. A car leaves Manila at 10 AM and arrives in Baguio City at 2PM. Suppose Baguio City is 270 km from Manila. What is the car's average speed?


Formula for speed:
Speed = distance/time

For meters per second (commonly used) :

10 AM to 2 PM = 4 hours
270km = distance

1 km = 1000m
1 hr = 60s
270 km = 27000m
4 hrs = 240s

Speed = 27000 m/240 s
Speed = 112.5 m/s

The average speed of the car is 112.5 m/s.

For kilometer per hour:

Speed = 270 km / 4 hours
Speed = 67.5 km/h

The average speed of the car is 67.5 km/h.

Distance = 270 km (Given)


Find the duration:

Duration = 10 am to 2pm

Duration = 4 hours


Find the speed:

Speed = Distance ÷ Time

Speed = 270 ÷ 4 = 67.5 km/h


Answer: The average speed is 67.5 km/h
