Drawing paper is a little heavier. Drawing papers are often heavier, allowing the artist to work on the sheet for extended periods of time and perfect their work. More erasing and fine detail work may normally be done on the paper.

The most common drawing paper problem is that it breaks easily. The two most common compass difficulties are air bubbles inside the compass housing and reversed polarity. If the T-square falls to the floor, it can shatter. When you use a sharp instrument on the top of the wooden drawing table, it will destroy it and leave holes.
