GCF of 18 and 27 is 9.

Step-by-step explanation:

The GCF of two non-zero integers, x(18) and y(27), is the greatest positive integer m(9) that divides both x(18) and y(27) without any remainder.

Solution:

Given: GCF (x, 18) = 9 and LCM (x, 18) = 54

∵ GCF × LCM = 18 × (x)

⇒ x = (GCF × LCM)/18

⇒ x = (9 × 54)/18

⇒ x = 27

Therefore, the other number is 27.
