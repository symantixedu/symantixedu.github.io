22. 340 is what percent of 442?​


Answer:

76.92

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution for 340 is what percent of 442:

340:442*100 =

(340*100):442 =

34000:442 = 76.92

Now we have: 340 is what percent of 442 = 76.92

Question: 340 is what percent of 442?

Percentage solution with steps:

Step 1: We make the assumption that 442 is 100% since it is our output value.

Step 2: We next represent the value we seek with x

Step 3: From step 1, it follows that

100%=442

Step 4: In the same vein, x%=340

Step 5: This gives us a pair of simple equations:

100%=442(1)

×%=340(2)

Step 6: By simply dividing equation 1 by equation 2 and taking note of the fact that both the LHS

(left hand side) of both equations have the same unit (%); we have

100%/×%=442/340

Step 7: Taking the inverse (or reciprocal) of both sides yields

×%/100%=340/442

=×= 76.92%

Therefore, 340 is 76.92% of 442
