30. State if each set of three numbers can be the lengths of the sides of a triangle. White Yes or No. Write your answers on a separate sheet of paper.


Answer:

Imagine lying out 2 sticks with lengths 9 and 12

on the ground. The maximum length you can

get is by putting them in a straight line. (9 + 12 = 21)

However, 21 is less than 24, which is

supposed to be the 3rd side of the triangle.

Hence the set 9, 12, 24 cannot form a triangle.
