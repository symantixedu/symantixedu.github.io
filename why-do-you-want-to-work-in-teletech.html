29. Why do you want to work at this company?


My answer for "Why do I want to work at the company (I applied)?"

“This company is known internationally for (the company's products) . Based on my search for this company, I will be able to inspire and make a positive contribution to the environment. Working for this company will give me the opportunity to build my skills and experience on the role (job position being applied for).”

EXPLANATION:

The question “why do you want to work for our company?” very often asked by HR during interviews. This question is not asked without purpose and is usually asked when the HRD/user is interested in your CV or resume. The purpose of HRD is to ask the question “reasons for working at the company” to gauge your interest in this job offer. In the selection process, HRD wants to recruit new employees who have a high level of interest. So that these employees can contribute in earnest.

Similar questions that are often asked by HRD regarding the reasons for working in a company are as follows:

why do you want to join us?why are you interested in working here?Why are you interested in working in our company?

Through this question, HRD also wants to know about your interview preparation. Did you do any research on the company or did you not. The smart candidate has the readiness and knowledge of the company.
