Thing being asked:

How many people are there in the garden?

The correct answer is 31 people (including me).

EXPLANATION:

The person who killed 30 people is still in the park. Plus me, being the 31 people that were in the park. Meanwhile, the 3 people who ran would definitely run from the park to escape and didn't want to be shot either.
