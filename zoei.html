We can even become an accountability buddy to Zoey. For example, we can set a routine that is done together at a certain time and we can check on each other if it has been done. Maybe after every lunchtime, we can brush our teeth and scrape our tongues together.

If the school has a dentist and a guidance counselor, we can definitely take advantage of that by scheduling a visit together. The key here is to make sure that Zoey does not feel alone and that the issues she has with her growing body is something that is normal and can be experienced by anyone. She just needs to know that there are people who can help her.
